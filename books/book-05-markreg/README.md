## Scenario and Surface Contract

PF-04 establishes:

- controlled scenarios `MR-SCN-01–41`;
- standard, high-risk and zero-tolerance severity;
- coverage of MR-CR-01 through MR-CR-08;
- client, professional, reviewer, approver, coordinator, finance, provider, administrator and AI roles;
- field- and purpose-specific visibility;
- role-switch and delegation controls;
- lifecycle surfaces from CH07 through CH47;
- Communication, embedded, Return and AI surface rules;
- minimum tests for eight conformance profiles.

The eight profiles are:

1. Foundation;
2. Guided Decision;
3. Commercial Intake;
4. Filing Preparation;
5. Governed Filing;
6. Post-Filing;
7. Portfolio Continuity;
8. Full-Lifecycle.

A profile cannot pass when an applicable zero-tolerance scenario fails.
