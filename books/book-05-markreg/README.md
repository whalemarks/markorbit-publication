## Controlled Reference Journeys

### `EMBERLOOP`

```text
Need and recommendations
→ exact-version commercial confirmation
→ governed filing through three routes
→ US examination
→ EU opposition
→ UK registration and maintenance
```

Final reviewed state:

- UK — registered with Right Baseline and maintenance obligations;
- US — under examination after acknowledged Response Package v2;
- EU — in opposition without assumed closure;
- Japan and Australia — candidates only.

### `RIVERKITE`

```text
six registrations
→ owner and deadline verification
→ four ordinary renewals
→ one ownership-linked renewal
→ one cancellation defense
→ evidence and licence actions
```

The initial three urgent deadlines are a subset of the wider five renewal-related workflows.
