## 3. Controlled Capitalization and Naming

Controlled uses of Product, Workplace, Core, Execution, Review, Decision, Approval, Communication, Document, Evidence, Knowledge, Capability, Order, Matter and Task were normalized. Lowercase remains for ordinary-language use.

Stable suffixes remain distinct:

| Suffix | Meaning |
| --- | --- |
| Record | retained controlled fact, Rule, Evidence or Decision identity |
| Snapshot | sourced state at a stated time |
| View | explainable Projection across records |
| Context | scoped facts and relationships for a journey or proceeding |
| Set | bounded collection with common purpose/version |
| Baseline | reviewed reference state |
| Envelope | typed transfer of references, versions, responsibility and expected result |
| Candidate | proposed work before required Review/Approval/action |

## 5. Jurisdiction, Commercial and AI Terms

Jurisdiction support is expressed through exact jurisdiction, service, stage, Pack Version and Support State.

Commercial components remain separated:

```text
Official Fee
≠ Mandatory Third-Party Cost
≠ Professional Fee
≠ Provider Pass-Through
≠ Internal Provider Cost
≠ Tax
≠ Currency Adjustment
≠ Discount / Credit
≠ Margin
```

AI language is limited to extraction, comparison, drafting, explanation, proposal, classification, flagging and summarization candidates. Eligible Humans Review, Decide, Approve and remain accountable.
