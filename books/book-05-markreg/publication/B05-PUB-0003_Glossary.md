# B05-PUB-0003 — Glossary

## Status

- **Status:** Controlled Scaffold
- **Primary workstreams:** PF-02, PF-05, PF-06 and PF-07
- **Applies to:** CH00–CH47 and Appendix A–G

## Purpose

This glossary provides reader-facing definitions of recurring Book 05 terms.

It does not replace Book 02 Core definitions or the controlled Book 05 specifications. Where a shared Core term is used, the Book 02 meaning remains authoritative.

## Initial Controlled Terms

### Acknowledgement

Evidence that an office, provider or system has received or recognized a submission or action. The exact source and procedural effect must be stated. Acknowledgement is not registration.

### Adversarial Context

The versioned Product context for an opposition, cancellation, invalidation or other contested proceeding, including parties, grounds, scope, evidence, authority, deadlines and outcome state.

### AI Assistance

Model-supported extraction, comparison, drafting, explanation, classification or issue detection performed inside a governed Product task. AI Assistance does not create professional responsibility, approval or official truth.

### Applicant

The legal or natural person identified as applying for a trademark right. Applicant is distinct from client, owner, payer, instructor, signatory and operating organization.

### Approval

A bounded Human authorization applying to an exact artifact version, purpose, scope and validity period. Approval is not Execution.

### Artifact

A versioned work product or representation with identity, source lineage, responsibility and supersession behavior. An Artifact may remain Product-local or may support later formalization.

### Business Context Snapshot

The versioned business, brand, market, timing, budget, risk and organization context used to begin or reassess a MarkReg journey.

### Capability Need

A typed statement of the professional or operational capability required for a jurisdiction, procedure, language, deadline, conflict and commercial context.

### Chain-of-Title View

A sourced view of ownership and transaction links from original applicant or owner through changes, mergers, assignments and current official record.

### Client Acceptance

The client’s recorded acceptance of an exact Quote version and stated commercial scope. It does not automatically create a formal Order or filing authority.

### Commercial Instruction

The authorized request to proceed within accepted commercial scope and conditions. It remains distinct from payment, formal Order, Matter, Filing Approval and Execution.

### Communication

A governed message or communication artifact with purpose, recipients, content version, approval where required, delivery state and correction history.

### Conformance Profile

A declared level of MarkReg capability support, such as Guided Decision, Commercial Intake, Filing Preparation or Full-Lifecycle, with explicit scope and exclusions.

### Decision

An accountable choice recorded with actor, authority, scope, basis, time and consequence. A recommendation or AI suggestion is not a Decision.

### Deadline Record

The controlled record of an official, calculated, estimated, internal or reminder date, including source, rule, event, confidence, responsibility and change history.

### Document

A formal or controlled document object with identity, version, source and permitted use. An uploaded file is not automatically a valid Document.

### Evidence

Material used to support a fact, use, authority, filing, argument, payment, submission or official outcome, with provenance, scope and purpose.

### Execution

The governed coordination of approved work under Book 03, including task, permission, idempotency, event, failure, retry and audit behavior.

### Filing Approval

Human authorization to execute an exact Filing Package version for a defined jurisdiction, route and purpose. It is invalidated by material change.

### Filing Package Candidate

The versioned Product artifact assembling proposed filing content, documents, declarations, scope and source lineage before formal filing.

### Formal Intake

The service-specific collection of facts needed for professional preparation after strategy and commercial scope are sufficiently defined.

### Handoff Envelope

A versioned, idempotent transfer of stable references, approved versions, responsibilities, deadlines, warnings and expected return evidence between Product and formal contexts.

### Human Judgment

Accountable professional or organizational judgment exercised by an eligible Human actor. It may be assisted but not replaced by AI.

### Human Selection

The accountable choice of provider or route from eligible candidates. A ranking or recommendation is not Human Selection.

### Jurisdiction Pack

A scoped and versioned configuration and Knowledge package defining sources, rules, fees, forms, requirements, Product behavior, professional ownership and change control for a jurisdiction/service combination.

### Maintenance Obligation Set

The versioned set of renewal, use, declaration, evidence, representation, fee, recordal and other continuing obligations associated with a right.

### Matter

A formal shared business or professional work context owned by its defined service. A Product journey may link to a Matter but does not silently create or redefine it.

### Need Brief

A concise, versioned statement of the business problem, intended protection, timing, priorities and constraints before formal filing data is collected.

### Official Event Snapshot

A sourced Product representation of an official notice, action, status or procedural event, including retrieval time, correction and interpretation context.

### Official Truth

The current authoritative fact established by the relevant office or official evidence. Product projection and provider report are not Official Truth without source support.

### Option Set

A versioned presentation of comparable alternatives, trade-offs, assumptions, exclusions, cost and next actions derived from recommendations.

### Order

A formal commercial object representing accepted service scope and related business terms. It remains distinct from Product acceptance, Matter and Execution.

### Outcome Snapshot

A sourced representation of the current result or procedural outcome, with scope, evidence, interpretation and continuing consequence.

### Owning Service

The service authorized to create or change a formal shared business fact. Displaying a fact does not make MarkReg its Owning Service.

### Portfolio Action Plan

A prioritized, reviewable set of proposed actions across independent rights, including rationale, authority, timing, cost and dependencies.

### Portfolio Continuity View

An explainable Product view across independent applications, registrations, disputes, obligations and opportunities without creating one global right or status.

### Product Projection

A Product-rendered interpretation or display of a source fact, official status or expected consequence. It must preserve source and uncertainty.

### Product Session

The current user interaction state with a MarkReg journey, including context, step, visible versions, warnings and resume conditions. It is not formal Matter state.

### Professional Review

A defined review by an eligible professional of an exact artifact version, source basis, risk, scope or proposed action.

### Proposal

A client- or professional-facing explanation of recommended service configuration, alternatives, assumptions, exclusions and next steps.

### Quote

A versioned commercial artifact stating price, scope, validity, currency, tax, inclusions, exclusions, assumptions, later-stage fees and payment terms.

### Readiness Assessment

A purpose-specific evaluation of structural, commercial, professional, documentary, payment, approval and execution conditions. Readiness is not approval.

### Recommendation Set

A versioned collection of jurisdiction, route, filing-unit, classification, goods/services, search, timing and risk recommendations with assumptions and source context.

### Recordal Context

The versioned context for an official-record change, including current and proposed values, authority, documents, sequence, package and official result.

### Requirement Set

The versioned set of documents, signatures, translations, certifications, originals, evidence, deadlines and jurisdiction-specific conditions required for a purpose.

### Response Package

The versioned set of response arguments, amendments, evidence, declarations, documents and filing content prepared for an official examination or proceeding.

### Return Envelope

The typed, idempotent result returned from MarkReg to a calling Product or Workplace, containing references, versions, outcomes, blockers and next-action candidates.

### Right Baseline

The sourced, reviewed baseline of a registered or continuing right, including official identifier, owner, scope, status, evidence and future obligations.

### Rule Record

A controlled operational rule with human-readable meaning, machine-readable condition where applicable, source, scope, effective date, exceptions, status, owner and tests.

### Submission Evidence

Technical, provider or official evidence showing what was sent, delivered, received or acknowledged. Its source determines the procedural claim it can support.

### Workplace

The independent organization-level operating environment and Orbit context defined by Book 04. MarkReg may operate within a Workplace without absorbing its authority.
