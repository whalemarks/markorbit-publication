## Figure Rules

Every figure should include or reference:

- figure ID and title;
- controlled source chapters or appendix;
- version and status;
- legend for Product, formal, Execution, provider and official contexts where applicable;
- independent jurisdiction or right treatment;
- Human Review and approval points;
- source and evidence boundaries;
- known simplifications.

## Visual Conventions

The final visual system should distinguish:

- Product-local artifacts;
- formal shared objects;
- Human decisions;
- governed Execution;
- external provider actions;
- official evidence and status;
- warnings, blockers and unknown states;
- Handoff and Return routes.

Color or shape must not be the only means of distinction.

## Reuse Rule

A figure may be referenced by several chapters and appendices.

Do not create near-duplicate figures merely to match every chapter. Prefer one controlled figure with a precise caption and chapter-specific explanatory text.

## Completion Work

PF-07 and PF-08 must:

1. create the registered Mermaid or other controlled source files;
2. review each figure against its source chapters;
3. add chapter and appendix references;
4. validate rendering in target formats;
5. test legibility in grayscale and accessible presentation;
6. record any simplification that could affect authority or state interpretation;
7. remove or merge figures that do not add reader value.

This register becomes final only when every retained figure has a controlled source file and rendered review evidence.
