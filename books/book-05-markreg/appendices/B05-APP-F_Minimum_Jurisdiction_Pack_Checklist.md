# Appendix F — Minimum Jurisdiction Pack Checklist

**Status:** Controlled Reader Draft v0.3 — PF-06D Reconciled  
**Primary source:** B05-SPEC-0004 v0.3  
**Reader purpose:** determine whether a jurisdiction, service and lifecycle stage is genuinely supported, at what level, and with what remaining gaps.

## F.1 Support Rule

```text
supported jurisdiction / service / stage
= scoped Pack module
+ reviewed Sources and Rules
+ professional owner
+ tested Product behavior
+ commercial controls
+ maintenance and suspension process
```

A country name, office link, fee table, provider profile or AI answer is not support.

## F.2 Pack Identity

- [ ] stable `MR-G01` Pack ID;
- [ ] immutable `MR-G03` Pack Version;
- [ ] jurisdiction and official authority;
- [ ] exact service family and lifecycle stages;
- [ ] applicant/owner types and routes;
- [ ] Source, official and Product languages;
- [ ] effective period;
- [ ] support state;
- [ ] exclusions and known gaps;
- [ ] professional owner and reviewers;
- [ ] `MR-D12 Pack Release Approval`;
- [ ] next-review trigger.

A multi-module Pack states each module independently.

## F.3 Support-State Checklist

### Research Only

- [ ] internal Source collection and comparison only;
- [ ] no client-facing current-rule claim;
- [ ] no conforming recommendation or Package claim;
- [ ] no protected action.

### Guidance Capable

- [ ] reviewed `MR-E07 Source Records` and `MR-G02 Rule Records`;
- [ ] effective and retrieval dates;
- [ ] limitations and uncertainty visible;
- [ ] professional escalation;
- [ ] applicable guidance scenarios pass.

### Preparation Capable

- [ ] all Guidance requirements;
- [ ] exact Requirement Set;
- [ ] Document, form and fee-unit Rules;
- [ ] Package schema;
- [ ] Review, blocker, override and material-diff behavior;
- [ ] preparation scenarios pass.

### Execution Capable

- [ ] all Preparation requirements;
- [ ] supported provider, connector or manual route;
- [ ] service-specific Approval contract;
- [ ] stable idempotency identity;
- [ ] Submission and acknowledgement Evidence;
- [ ] unknown-state reconciliation and duplicate safety;
- [ ] operational owner and disable mechanism.

### Lifecycle Capable

- [ ] official-event continuation;
- [ ] Deadlines and responsibility;
- [ ] outcome mapping;
- [ ] maintenance or later-stage obligations;
- [ ] exact declared renewal, recordal, dispute or portfolio continuation;
- [ ] historical versions;
- [ ] Source-change and suspension process.

### Suspended / Retired

- [ ] reason, affected modules and new-action restriction;
- [ ] open-Matter continuation plan;
- [ ] historical version retained;
- [ ] reactivation or replacement condition.

## F.4 Source and Rule Checklist

### `MR-E07 Source Record`

- [ ] authority/author, type, title, jurisdiction and language;
- [ ] official location or immutable reference;
- [ ] publication, effective and retrieval dates;
- [ ] version/checksum and supersession;
- [ ] confidentiality/use restriction;
- [ ] reviewer and interpretation note.

### `MR-G02 Rule Record`

- [ ] stable Rule ID and Human-readable statement;
- [ ] machine condition where used;
- [ ] affected stages, inputs and output/warning/blocker;
- [ ] Source references and exceptions;
- [ ] effective period and status;
- [ ] professional owner;
- [ ] scenarios and history.

Only permitted active states may drive protected behavior.

## F.5 New Filing Checklist

- [ ] `MR-C12 Applicant and Authority Context` requirements;
- [ ] eligibility, owner, priority, instructor and signatory Rules;
- [ ] direct, regional and international routes;
- [ ] representation requirement;
- [ ] mark types and files;
- [ ] classification edition and local deviations;
- [ ] goods/services practice and fee units;
- [ ] search options and limits;
- [ ] Official Fee, Professional Fee and later-stage components;
- [ ] POA, signature, original, notarization, legalization and translation;
- [ ] form and Filing Package schema;
- [ ] provider/connector/manual route;
- [ ] acknowledgement identifiers;
- [ ] correction, rejection and later-stage behavior.

## F.6 Post-Filing and Dispute Checklist

### Examination and response

- [ ] official event types and Evidence;
- [ ] issue categories and affected scope;
- [ ] response/amendment options;
- [ ] Evidence and declarations;
- [ ] official Deadline basis and extensions;
- [ ] Response Package schema;
- [ ] Professional Review and client Decision;
- [ ] filing, acknowledgement, remedy and outcome mapping;
- [ ] stage-specific fees.

### Publication, opposition and adversarial work

- [ ] publication Evidence and challenge window;
- [ ] parties, standing, pleadings and Evidence stages;
- [ ] extensions/cooling-off;
- [ ] provider Capability and conflict;
- [ ] settlement authority;
- [ ] official/provider/professional fee stages;
- [ ] appeal, cancellation or invalidation routes;
- [ ] official closure Evidence;
- [ ] independent Matter and Deadline behavior.

## F.7 Registration and Maintenance Checklist

- [ ] Registration Outcome and registered scope;
- [ ] Filing and Scope Diff View;
- [ ] certificate availability and certificate/register distinction;
- [ ] registration-stage fees;
- [ ] use, declaration and Evidence duties;
- [ ] local-representation continuity;
- [ ] official/calculated/internal/reminder dates;
- [ ] current official owner/address verification;
- [ ] Right Baseline update;
- [ ] vulnerability and escalation.

## F.8 Renewal Checklist

- [ ] ordinary, early, grace and restoration windows;
- [ ] official Deadline Source;
- [ ] right, class, scope and current owner verification;
- [ ] `MR-C12` where owner, instructor or signatory is material;
- [ ] partial renewal or limitation;
- [ ] use/Evidence dependencies;
- [ ] Documents and signatures;
- [ ] ordinary, surcharge, provider and professional fees;
- [ ] Renewal Package Candidate;
- [ ] Renewal Approval;
- [ ] payment and Execution route;
- [ ] official acknowledgement and Official Update Evidence;
- [ ] updated Right Baseline.

## F.9 Changes, Transactions and Licence Checklist

- [ ] current, claimed and proposed official values;
- [ ] affected rights;
- [ ] forms and supporting Documents;
- [ ] signatures, authority, certification, notarization, legalization and translation;
- [ ] sequencing with renewal or dispute;
- [ ] partial official results and correction behavior;
- [ ] transaction type, parties, authority and effective date;
- [ ] chain-of-title dependencies;
- [ ] contractual effect versus official effect;
- [ ] licence scope, quality control, use and expiry;
- [ ] official/provider/professional fees;
- [ ] Official Update Evidence.

## F.10 Portfolio and Monitoring Checklist

- [ ] supported official and monitoring Sources;
- [ ] independent-right preservation;
- [ ] signal versus official-event distinction;
- [ ] Deadline and obligation aggregation;
- [ ] coverage-gap and Evidence indicators;
- [ ] action candidates and Non-Renewal Decision support;
- [ ] no automatic infringement conclusion;
- [ ] no protected action from signal alone.

## F.11 Deadline, Form and Change Checklist

- [ ] triggering event, Source hierarchy and event/receipt distinction;
- [ ] calculation, timezone, calendar and extension/grace Rules;
- [ ] official, calculated, internal and reminder dates separated;
- [ ] responsible reviewer and conservative conflict handling;
- [ ] form/schema ID, version, authority, purpose and effective date;
- [ ] fields, declarations, signature, attachments, formats and route;
- [ ] superseded version retained;
- [ ] affected open/approved work identified;
- [ ] re-signature/reconfirmation Rule;
- [ ] `MR-G04 Rule Change Candidate` and `MR-D12 Pack Release Approval` used.

## F.12 Commercial Checklist

- [ ] Official Fee separated;
- [ ] Mandatory Third-Party Cost separated;
- [ ] Professional Fee separated;
- [ ] Provider Pass-Through separated;
- [ ] Internal Provider Cost private;
- [ ] Tax and Currency Adjustment visible when charged;
- [ ] Contingency and Later-Stage Fees described;
- [ ] Discount/Credit authority recorded;
- [ ] Margin private and never labelled Official Fee;
- [ ] immutable Quote version;
- [ ] fee-driving units and effective date;
- [ ] variance, repricing, refund and supplementary-payment path;
- [ ] client, finance, professional and provider visibility tested.

## F.13 AI and Conformance Checklist

- [ ] `MR-G06 AI Task Context` contains purpose-required Sources and limits;
- [ ] `MR-G07 AI Output Record` retains basis, uncertainty and Human disposition;
- [ ] model memory is not current law;
- [ ] AI cannot release Pack, Review, approve or execute;
- [ ] scenario set passes for claimed Profile;
- [ ] Support Claim does not exceed module Support State;
- [ ] `MR-G10 Conformance Statement` identifies exclusions and authority state;
- [ ] suspension and stop behavior tested.
