# Appendix A — Full-Lifecycle Artifact and Decision Map

**Status:** Controlled Scaffold — content completion pending PF-02  
**Primary sources:** B05-SPEC-0001; CH08–CH47  
**Reader purpose:** provide one compact map of the Product artifacts and accountable decisions used across MarkReg.

## A.1 Reading Rule

This appendix is a reader projection of controlled specifications.

```text
Artifact = a versioned Product or formal work product
Decision = an accountable choice or approval
State = what is currently true about an artifact or process
Evidence = what supports a fact, action or outcome
```

One term may appear in several views, but its authority does not change merely because it is displayed in an appendix.

## A.2 Canonical Journey Lineage

```text
Business Context
→ Need Brief
→ Recommendation Set
→ Option Set
→ Proposal
→ Quote
→ Client Acceptance
→ Commercial Instruction
→ Formal Intake
→ Requirement Set
→ Readiness Assessment

→ Filing Approval
→ Provider or Execution Instruction

→ Official Acknowledgement
```

Not every service uses every artifact. Renewal, recordal, opposition and cancellation journeys may enter at later points while preserving equivalent source, version, authority and evidence controls.

## A.4 Decision Families

| Decision | Accountable actor | Applies to | Does not equal |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| user factual confirmation | client or authorized fact provider | stated facts and selected options | Professional Review |
| Client Acceptance | authorized client actor | an exact Quote and commercial scope | Commercial Instruction for a changed scope |
| Commercial Instruction | authorized client or organization actor | request to proceed within accepted scope | formal Order, payment or filing |
| Professional Decision | eligible professional | recommendation, strategy, evidence or override | client authority or Execution |
| Professional Review | eligible reviewer | a defined artifact version | Filing Approval or submission |
| internal commercial approval | eligible organization role | price, discount or risk policy | client acceptance |
| Filing Approval | authorized Human role | exact package, jurisdiction and route | Execution or official filing |
| Human Selection | authorized organization actor | provider or route choice | provider appointment or acceptance |
| Provider Acceptance | appointed provider | accepted engagement and scope | official submission |
| settlement or remedy decision | authorized client and professional roles | adversarial strategy and authority | official procedural closure |
| renewal or recordal approval | authorized Human role | exact later-stage package | renewed or updated official right |
| non-renewal decision | authorized owner or client role | a specific right and consequence | lapse before official effect occurs |
| conformance decision | publication or implementation reviewer | declared profile and evidence | implementation or production authority |

## A.5 Version and Supersession Rule

Every material artifact should retain:

- stable ID;
- version ID;
- source references;
- responsible actor;
- created and updated time;
- Review and approval state;
- validity or expiry condition;
- superseded-by reference;
- downstream artifacts already created from it.

A changed upstream fact does not silently mutate an accepted Quote, approved package, formal Order, Matter, instruction, submission or official record.

## A.6 Formalization Boundary

The appendix must continue to distinguish:

```text
Product-local candidate
≠ formal shared object
≠ Execution state
≠ provider report
≠ official record
```

Formal Order, Matter, Document, Review, approval, payment and other shared facts remain owned by their defined services.
