## Reader Order

The appendices follow CH47 in this order:

```text
Appendix A — artifacts and decisions
→ Appendix B — states and authority
→ Appendix C — participant rights
→ Appendix D — reference journeys
→ Appendix E — conformance scenarios
→ Appendix F — jurisdiction-pack checklist
→ Appendix G — conformance profiles
```

The publication apparatus follows or supports the appendices through Source and Authority Notes, Glossary, Subject Index, Figure Register, Cross-Book Reconciliation and RC1 Checklist.

## Generation Rule

Each appendix must identify:

- controlled source documents;
- source version or review baseline;
- included and excluded concepts;
- reader-facing simplifications;
- last reconciliation review;
- known gaps;
- supersession behavior.

Appendices may summarize. They may not:

- create a new constitutional rule;
- redefine a Core object;
- grant Review, approval or Execution authority;
- convert Product projection into official truth;
- generalize one provider’s advice into a jurisdiction rule;
- hide independent jurisdiction or right states.
