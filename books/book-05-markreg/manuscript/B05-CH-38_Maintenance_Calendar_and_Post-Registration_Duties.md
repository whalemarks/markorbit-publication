# B05-CH-38 — Maintenance Calendar and Post-Registration Duties

**Status:** Complete Draft 1  
**Chapter Map:** B05-TOC-V0.1 — Owner Accepted  
**Part:** Part VI — Registration and Portfolio Continuity

## Chapter Purpose

A registered right creates continuing duties, not one future renewal date.

```text
EL-31 / CH38
MR-B01 Right Baseline
→ MR-B02 Maintenance Obligation Set
→ MR-B03 Use-Evidence Coverage Record
→ MR-E06 Deadline Records
→ responsibility and next action
```

The Product question is:

> What must be done to preserve this right, what evidence should exist, when is action due, and who is accountable?

```text
Reminder
≠ official deadline
≠ responsibility
≠ completed obligation
≠ continued validity
```

## 1. Maintenance Obligation Set

`MR-B02 Maintenance Obligation Set` should record each obligation separately:

- right, jurisdiction and obligation type;
- triggering event and sourced rule;
- opening, ordinary, late and restoration dates;
- required documents, declarations or evidence;
- affected classes or goods/services;
- responsible client, professional, evidence, finance and deadline owners;
- Provider or representative requirement;
- commercial conditions;
- current state and consequence of omission;
- uncertainty, conflict and supersession.

Maintenance may include use declarations, evidence, renewal, registration-stage fees, representative requirements, address-for-service duties, office requests and ownership corrections.

## 2. Deadline Authority

`MR-E06 Deadline Record` distinguishes:

```text
official stated date
calculated date
Provider-reported date
internal safety date
reminder date
grace-period end
```

Every calculation preserves its source, Pack and Rule version, time zone, holiday treatment, verifier and prior superseded result.

`MR-SCN-27` applies when Provider advice conflicts with a current official source: preserve both, use a conservative internal target and require Professional Review.

## 3. Windows and Status

An obligation may be:

```text
not yet open
planned
evidence collection
preparation active
waiting for client or Provider
ready for Review
approved
filed
acknowledged
completed
late
grace period
restoration required
missed
unknown
```

Each state belongs to one identified obligation. A generic green portfolio status must not hide a late duty or active vulnerability.

## 4. Use Evidence and Vulnerability

`MR-B03 Use-Evidence Coverage Record` maps:

- goods/services and mark version;
- territory and period;
- official owner, licensee or other using entity;
- sales, services, advertising and market materials;
- source, custodian and date;
- confidentiality and sharing permission;
- licence or group-use relationship;
- professional inclusion and sufficiency assessment;
- gaps and next collection action.

Evidence existence is not legal sufficiency. No immediate filing duty does not mean the right is safe from non-use challenge.

## 5. Responsibility and Escalation

A reminder does not transfer responsibility. Every material duty should identify:

- accountable deadline owner and backup;
- professional owner;
- client decision owner;
- evidence and document owners;
- finance and Provider coordinators;
- escalation threshold and consequence.

The Product and AI may create signals, but they cannot be the accountable owner.

## 6. Commercial and Pack Changes

Maintenance work may depend on current official fees, Provider pricing, representative status, owner correction, evidence review, payment and local formalities.

A new fee or Rule does not rewrite a completed action. It triggers impact analysis for open obligations, Quotes and Packages under PF-05 controls.

## 7. `EMBERLOOP` — `EL-31`

The UK `MR-B01 Right Baseline` produces:

- a future renewal window;
- a non-use vulnerability milestone;
- representative and address verification;
- an annual evidence-review recommendation.

No immediate official filing is due. The Product shows:

```text
next official filing: not yet open
evidence review: active recommendation
owner verification: complete
representative verification: complete
```

The EU opposition and US examination remain governed by their independent Part V contexts.

## 8. `RIVERKITE` — `RK-09`

Six independent Maintenance Obligation Sets reveal:

- four ordinary renewals approaching;
- one ownership-linked renewal;
- one cancellation-defense right;
- weak use evidence for two classes;
- a licence approaching expiry;
- Provider Quotes excluding late fees.

`RK-09` maps weak evidence by goods, territory, period, source and using entity. Vulnerability remains a professional assessment, not a conclusion that the right is invalid.

## 9. Controlled Scenarios

- **MR-SCN-10 — Stale official status:** refresh before relying on the status for a protected action.
- **MR-SCN-26 — Corrected notice:** recalculate duties and preserve the prior basis.
- **MR-SCN-27 — Conflicting deadline advice:** preserve hierarchy and require verification.
- **MR-SCN-31 — Partial renewal or owner mismatch:** expose the ownership or scope dependency before preparation proceeds.
- **MR-SCN-33 — One challenged right:** keep the cancellation defense separate from unrelated maintenance work.

## 10. AI Assistance

AI may extract approved Pack rules, calculate candidate dates, classify evidence and draft reminders. It must not establish the controlling deadline alone, decide evidence sufficiency, declare continued validity or authorize filing or payment.

## 11. Minimum Maintenance Lock

```text
Maintenance is obligation-specific.

Official dates, calculated dates,
internal targets and reminders
remain distinct.

Use, ownership, dispute,
evidence and renewal states
remain independently visible.

Every obligation has a source,
owner, consequence and next action.
```

## 12. Handoff to Renewal

CH39 converts an eligible obligation into `MR-A26 Renewal Package Candidate` and requires `MR-D08 Renewal Approval`, governed Execution and sourced official acknowledgement.
