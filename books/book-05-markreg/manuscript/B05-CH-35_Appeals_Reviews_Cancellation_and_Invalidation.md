# B05-CH-35 — Appeals, Reviews, Cancellation and Invalidation

**Status:** Complete Draft 1  
**Chapter Map:** B05-TOC-V0.1 — Owner Accepted

## Chapter Purpose

A sourced decision or challenge may create a separate remedy journey. The available route depends on the decision, forum, standing, deadline and applicable Jurisdiction Pack.

CH35 answers:

> Which remedy, defense or correction path is actually available, who may pursue it, what must be preserved, and what outcome has been sourced?

```text
verified decision or challenge
→ MR-C06 Remedy Context
→ sourced Remedy Options
→ accountable Decision
→ governed package and filing
→ sourced remedy outcome
```

Appeal, administrative review, judicial review, correction, restoration, cancellation and invalidation are not one generic procedure.

## 1. User Question and Primary Action

**User question:** What decision or challenge exists, which paths are available and by when must we decide?

**Primary action:** Approve a sourced Remedy Strategy, choose not to proceed or authorize a bounded preservation action.

The Product must not present a remedy as available or filed without the required source and acknowledgement.

## 2. Remedy Context

`MR-C06 Remedy Context` references:

- official decision, challenge or petition;
- affected right, scope and formal Matter;
- parties, provider and representation;
- decision, service and retrieval dates;
- remedy routes, forum and eligibility;
- standing, ownership and authority;
- official and internal deadlines;
- record and new-Evidence rules;
- fees, security and local-counsel needs;
- Decisions, Packages, filings and outcomes.

```text
Remedy Context
≠ formal proceeding
≠ official case file
≠ guarantee of availability
≠ reversal or successful defense
```

## 3. Proceeding and Option Distinctions

The Product preserves distinct routes for:

- correction or reconsideration;
- administrative or judicial review;
- appeal;
- restoration or revival;
- cancellation, revocation or invalidation;
- non-use challenge;
- official-record correction.

Each option must show source-backed availability, standing, deadline, scope, Evidence rules, interim effect, cost, provider and authority.

A Remedy Strategy is not a filed remedy, accepted appeal, stay or reversal.

## 4. Standing, Ownership and Authority

Before instruction or filing, verify:

- official applicant or owner;
- chain of title and recordal status;
- party status in the prior proceeding;
- signatory and instructor authority;
- territorial or procedural standing;
- live status of the right and deadline;
- settlement, waiver or prior-conduct effects;
- required local professional or security.

A business ownership claim does not automatically establish recorded ownership or procedural standing.

## 5. Record, Evidence and Urgent Preservation

The Context states whether the remedy uses the existing record, limited new Evidence or a new evidentiary record.

Where a deadline requires preservation before the complete strategy is approved, a bounded action may be used only under documented policy. It must state purpose, cost, scope preserved, authority, further steps and stop conditions.

Urgent preservation does not commit the client to the full proceeding.

## 6. Cancellation and Invalidation

A challenge to an existing registration must preserve:

- current Right Baseline and official owner;
- challenged and unaffected goods/services;
- grounds and source petition;
- use, maintenance, assignment and licence context;
- standing and representation;
- response deadline and Evidence burden;
- limitation, settlement and defense options;
- parallel renewal or recordal Matters.

A challenged registration is not cancelled until a sourced official outcome establishes that effect.

## 7. Parallel Proceedings

Remedy, cancellation, renewal, recordal, settlement and other proceedings may be linked, but they retain separate:

- Matters and parties;
- deadlines and providers;
- confidentiality and Evidence permissions;
- budgets and Decisions;
- filings and official outcomes.

One decision does not silently determine another proceeding.

## 8. `EMBERLOOP` — `EL-28`

No separate EMBERLOOP appeal, review, cancellation or invalidation remedy is activated in the reviewed baseline.

The EU opposition remains active under CH34. The Product does not invent a later adverse decision merely to demonstrate an appeal workflow.

If a sourced future decision creates a remedy, a new Remedy Context and Decision will be required.

## 9. `RIVERKITE` — `RK-03` and `RK-07`

One of six registrations receives a cancellation challenge affecting selected goods.

MarkReg records:

- sourced cancellation event and Remedy Context;
- challenged and unaffected scope;
- current official owner versus business-owner conflict;
- defense, Evidence and response deadlines;
- provider, staged Quote and responsibility;
- recordal and renewal links as separate Matters.

The professional recommends preserving the deadline, resolving instructor authority, defending Evidence-supported core goods, considering limitation of unsupported peripheral goods and coordinating recordal without assuming retroactive cure.

The cancellation defense remains active. No official cancellation or defense victory is assumed.

## 10. Controlled Scenarios

- `MR-SCN-26` — corrected decision or challenge source;
- `MR-SCN-27` — conflicting remedy deadline advice;
- `MR-SCN-28` — silence does not authorize appeal, abandonment or settlement;
- `MR-SCN-32` — signed assignment is not recorded ownership;
- `MR-SCN-33` — one challenged right remains independent from the rest of the portfolio.

## 11. AI Assistance

AI may summarize a decision, compare remedy routes, organize the record, flag ownership conflicts and prepare a decision brief.

AI may not establish standing, invent a remedy or deadline, decide Evidence admissibility, authorize preservation, file a challenge, claim a stay, predict reversal or mark a challenged right cancelled without official evidence.
