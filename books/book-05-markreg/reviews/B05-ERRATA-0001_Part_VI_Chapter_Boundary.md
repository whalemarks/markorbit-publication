# B05-ERRATA-0001 — Part VI Chapter Boundary and Titles

## Status

- **Status:** Effective correction
- **Affected record:** B05-REV-0009 and downstream status records created from it
- **Authority:** B05-TOC-V0.1 — Owner Accepted
- **Effective scope:** Book 05 Part VI and Part VII continuation gates

## Error

B05-REV-0009 incorrectly stated that Part VI covered CH37–CH43 and listed non-canonical chapter titles.

The owner-accepted chapter map has always defined:

```text
Part VI — CH37–CH42
Part VII — CH43–CH47
```

Canonical Part VI chapters are:

1. CH37 — Registration and Certificate Boundaries
2. CH38 — Maintenance Calendar and Post-Registration Duties
3. CH39 — Renewal Preparation and Governed Renewal
4. CH40 — Changes, Recordals and Ownership Updates
5. CH41 — Assignment, Licensing and Chain of Title
6. CH42 — Portfolio Strategy, Monitoring and Lifecycle Learning

CH43 is the first chapter of Part VII:

- CH43 — Standalone, Embedded and Cross-Product Experience

## Correction Rule

Where B05-REV-0009 or a derived status record conflicts with B05-TOC-V0.1 on the Part VI boundary or titles, this erratum and B05-TOC-V0.1 control.

The Part V substantive review result remains valid. Only the next-pack range and chapter-title list are corrected.

## Impact

- no chapter renumbering;
- no chapter-map amendment;
- no architecture change;
- no Book 02, Book 03 or Book 04 change;
- Part VI drafting proceeds as CH37–CH42;
- Part VII begins at CH43.
