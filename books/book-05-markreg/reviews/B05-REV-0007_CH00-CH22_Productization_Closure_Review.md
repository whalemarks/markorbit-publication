4. CH27 must distinguish direct connector, provider filing and Owning Service authority.
5. CH28 must preserve prepared, queued, sent, accepted, rejected, acknowledged and unknown states.
6. CH29 must define idempotency, duplicate safety, partial failure, retry, communication and audit evidence.
7. EMBERLOOP must continue through the Part IV chapters.
8. Every protected action must have a Given/When/Then conformance scenario and Human/authority boundary.
9. Jurisdiction-pack versions and source evidence remain normative.
10. Part IV must not recreate a Product-owned Workflow engine or official status authority.
