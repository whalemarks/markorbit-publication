## 5. Product Lineage

Part III now follows the canonical lineage:

```text

→ Proposal

→ Client Acceptance
→ Commercial Instruction
→ Formal Intake
```

Every downstream artifact identifies exact upstream versions. Material changes identify affected artifacts and do not silently mutate accepted Quotes, formal Orders, Matters or later approved packages.

## 6. Commercial Controls

The revised chapters now explicitly control:

- official, professional and provider fee separation;
- internal cost and margin visibility;
- fee-driving units;
- source and effective-date evidence;
- quote, invoice, payment and remittance currencies;
- exchange-rate policy and repricing threshold;
- tax-inclusive or tax-exclusive presentation;
- discount and exception authority;
- deposit, staged payment and credit terms;
- official-fee changes after acceptance;
- later-stage fees;
- cancellation, refund and failed-filing treatment.

No amount may be silently changed after acceptance.

## 7. User Experience Result

- the user question being resolved;

- next-state explanation.

The chapters do not use one generic progress percentage and do not require the user to understand architecture terminology before taking the next action.
