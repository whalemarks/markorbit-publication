## 1. Review Purpose

This review determines whether Book 05 now has one controlled Product record and decision model covering CH07–CH47 rather than a register that stops near filing preparation.

It tests whether the revised specification:

1. covers every material lifecycle stage;
2. preserves existing controlled identifiers where possible;
3. distinguishes artifacts from contexts, evidence, baselines, views and governance records;
4. records accountable Decisions separately from Product recommendations and readiness;
5. preserves formal object, Execution, provider and official-office boundaries;
6. defines source, Review, approval, version and supersession behavior;
7. supports multiple service-family entry points;
8. reconciles Appendix A and Appendix B without giving them independent authority;
9. leaves the eight MarkReg constitutional rules unchanged;
10. avoids an upstream semantic amendment.

## 4. Controlled Record Classes

The revised specification defines:

```text
MR-A — Product artifact
MR-C — scoped context
MR-D — accountable decision
MR-E — evidence or source-backed record
MR-B — baseline or obligation record
MR-V — view or projection
MR-G — governance and configuration record
```

This resolves the prior tendency to call every named Product concept an Artifact.

The class indicates meaning only. It does not grant authority.

**Result:** PASS.

1. new filing;

## 7. Decision and Authority Review

- Client Acceptance;
- Professional Decision;
