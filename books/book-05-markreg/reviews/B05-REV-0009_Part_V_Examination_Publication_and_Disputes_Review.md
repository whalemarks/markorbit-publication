## 12. Reference Journey Continuity

### `EMBERLOOP`

- US: examination notice → Issue Set → Response Strategy → Response Package v2 → official acknowledgement → examination continuing.
- EU: publication → verified opposition → Adversarial Context → defense and bounded negotiation; no unsupported settlement or closure.
- UK: publication window closes with no challenge found; registration-stage candidate opens without creating a Registration outcome.

### `RIVERKITE`

- one registration receives a cancellation challenge;
- official and business ownership conflict remains visible;
- defense, recordal and renewal remain linked but separate;
- evidence is mapped by goods, territory, date, source and owner;
- unaffected registrations retain independent states.
