1. defines one Product contract across standalone, Lite, Workplace and cross-Product surfaces;

- rule status and effective periods;
- change detection and impact assessment;

A detected source change creates a candidate, not an automatic active rule.

**Result:** PASS.

## 7. AI Assistance

Part VII consistently permits AI to assist with:

- explanation;
- comparison;
- missing-information detection;
- rule-change analysis;
- evaluation and feedback clustering;
- conformance review;
- publication editing.

It prohibits AI from:

- inventing sources;
- presenting memory as current law;
- granting permission or approval;
- replacing eligible professional judgment;
- expanding purpose or access;
- authorizing deployment or protected action.

Material AI outputs retain task, source, Pack, model, time and Human Review context.

**Result:** PASS.

## 8. Metrics and Quality

CH46 separates:

- user understanding;
- journey progression;
- professional quality;
- governance safety;
- operational reliability;
- commercial clarity;
- lifecycle continuity;
- reviewed learning.

It rejects blind optimization for application volume, class count, automation rate, completion speed, provider price or unsegmented registration success.

Safety interventions and professional overrides remain measurable positive signals where appropriate.

**Result:** PASS.

## 9. MVP Delivery Sequence

CH46 defines a progressive sequence:

```text
MVP 0 — Need Brief through governed Handoff
MVP 1 — Filing Package, Review, Approval and acknowledgement
MVP 2 — selected post-filing continuity
MVP 3 — portfolio, network and broader jurisdiction depth
```

The MVP is a narrow vertical Product loop rather than a shallow global feature catalogue.

Release gates, pilot controls, stop conditions, manual fallback and disable capability are explicit.

**Result:** PASS.
