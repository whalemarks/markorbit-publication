# B05-PLN-0003 — Chapter Map and Writing Gates

## 1. Chapter Map Candidate

B05-TOC-V0.1 proposes 48 manuscript files, CH00–CH47, across seven parts.

The detailed map is maintained in `manuscript/B05-CH-01_Table_of_Contents.md`.

## 2. Structural Logic

```text
Product Constitution
→ Need and Strategy
→ Commercial Journey and Intake
→ Filing Preparation and Execution
→ Examination and Disputes
→ Registration and Portfolio Continuity
→ Product Experience and Evolution
```

## 3. Writing Packs

### Pack 01 — Constitution

CH00–CH07

Purpose: establish why MarkReg exists, its mission, Product position, actors, principles, boundaries, and lifecycle distinctions.

### Pack 02 — Need and Recommendation

CH08–CH15

Purpose: define the AI-assisted and professionally governed journey before formal intake.

### Pack 03 — Commercial Journey and Intake

CH16–CH22

Purpose: convert strategy into understandable options, commercial confirmation, sufficient intake, and formal handoff.

### Pack 04 — Filing Preparation and Execution

CH23–CH29

Purpose: define filing-package preparation, Review, provider collaboration, submission states, failure, and trace.

### Pack 05 — Examination and Disputes

CH30–CH36

Purpose: define post-filing professional journeys, deadlines, evidence, response, and adversarial matters.

### Pack 06 — Registration and Portfolio Continuity

CH37–CH42

Purpose: define registration, maintenance, renewal, recordals, transactions, monitoring, and learning.

### Pack 07 — Product Experience and Evolution

CH43–CH47

Purpose: define surfaces, jurisdiction packs, AI, metrics, MVP, conformance, and roadmap.

## 4. Chapter Acceptance Test

Every chapter should answer:

1. What user or professional problem exists?
2. Which Product responsibility belongs to MarkReg?
3. Which responsibilities remain outside MarkReg?
4. Which facts, candidates, recommendations, and formal states must remain distinct?
5. Where does Human Review apply?
6. Where does governed Execution begin?
7. Which outcome returns to the Product?
8. Which failure modes must be rejected?

## 5. Map Gate

Before Pack 01 continues beyond CH03, the owner should confirm or revise:

- Book title;
- CH00–CH47 range;
- seven-part structure;
- inclusion of search and risk support;
- inclusion of adversarial and post-registration journeys;
- separation of MarkReg from all-IP expansion.

## 6. Drafting Rule

One branch may contain a coherent writing pack. Do not create one branch per chapter.

## 7. Non-Authorization

Acceptance of the map authorizes controlled manuscript drafting only. It does not authorize Product implementation or external protected action.
