## Canonical terminology lock

| Term | Controlled meaning | Must not collapse into |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Organization | accountable legal, commercial, professional or administrative participant | Workplace, Product, user account |
| Workplace | Organization-scoped business-sovereignty, accountability, permission, Product-operation and projection boundary | platform shell, generic dashboard, Product |
| Business sovereignty | control of customer relationship, private commercial context, internal Knowledge and accountable business responsibility | semantic authority, physical hosting |
| Semantic authority | authority over controlled meaning, identifiers, states and contracts | ownership of each concrete record |
| Formal-state authority | authority to create or mutate formal business facts through the applicable Owning Service | Workplace visibility or Product interaction |
| Physical custody | storage, transmission, processing and infrastructure administration | business sovereignty or legal rights |
| Legal / source authority | legal rights, official-source authority, evidentiary authority and professional responsibility | platform administration |
| Product Installation | Workplace-scoped Product identity, entitlement, configuration, permission and projection relationship | separate codebase, separate database, Product itself |
| Product Projection | purpose-limited view or interaction surface from authorized context | Handoff, Return, ownership transfer |
| Handoff | typed transfer of authorized context toward another authority boundary | destination acceptance or execution |
| Return | typed outcome, Evidence or status projection brought back to an originating context | automatic formal-state mutation |
| MGSN Connection / Gateway | Workplace-scoped interface to MGSN | MGSN provider pool or network ownership |
| MGSN Network | platform-owned and platform-governed managed professional-service network | participant-owned peer network or open marketplace |
| Originating Workplace | Workplace retaining originating customer, commercial and approval context | Execution Provider Workplace |
| Execution Provider Workplace | Workplace responsible for provider-side execution and professional responsibility | originating customer relationship ownership |
| Sites | Workplace public-brand and content projection Product | Customer, Order, Matter or Payment Owning Service |
