# B04-PLN-0004 — Chapter Writing Gates

## Gate 0 — Scaffold

This task.

Required result:

```text
Identity
Scope
Governance
Dependencies
Provisional TOC
Writing gates
```

## Gate 1 — Owner TOC Review

Required before chapter drafting.

## Gate 2 — Chapter Briefs

Each chapter must receive:

- purpose;
- questions answered;
- upstream authorities;
- required distinctions;
- examples;
- non-goals;
- expected cross-references;
- review conditions.

## Gate 3 — Writing-Pack Plan

Group chapters into controlled packs.

Do not default to one branch per chapter.

Recommended future pack direction:

```text
Pack 1: Front Matter and Part I
Pack 2: Part II
Pack 3: Part III
Pack 4: Part IV
Pack 5: Part V
Pack 6: Part VI
```

This grouping is provisional.

## Gate 4 — Controlled Drafting

Only after owner acceptance of the chapter map and briefs.

## Gate 5 — Part Reviews

Architecture, terminology and cross-book checks.

## Gate 6 — Full-Book Review

Consistency, compression, links, editorial quality and owner acceptance.

## Gate 7 — Publication Readiness

Separate from implementation readiness and protected-action authority.
