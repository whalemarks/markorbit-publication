## Required profiles

### MarkReg
MarkReg may operate as a Product Installation inside an existing Workplace. Where MarkReg serves its own direct customers as the accountable business operator, that self-operated business must be represented as an isolated Workplace rather than as a privileged Product-owned exception.

### Lite
Lite is a lightweight Workplace Product. It may expose Today, recommendation, task, communication or client-facing projections, but it does not become the Workplace, the Owning Service or the authority over projected facts.

### Sites
Sites is a public-brand and content Projection Product. It may publish authorized public content and intake surfaces. It does not own Customer, Order, Matter, Payment or professional-service records merely because they are initiated through a Site.

### MGSN
MGSN Connection / Gateway is the Workplace-scoped interface through which a participant discovers, evaluates and collaborates. MGSN Network is the platform-owned and platform-governed professional-service network. A connection does not make the participant owner of the network, and network participation does not make the platform owner of the participant's customer relationship.

## Cross-Workplace model

```text
Originating Workplace
→ purpose-limited Handoff
→ Execution Provider Workplace
→ governed execution
→ typed Return
→ Originating Workplace acceptance
```

The Originating Workplace retains originating customer, commercial, approval and relationship context. The Execution Provider Workplace carries provider-side execution, professional and evidence responsibilities. Neither role silently collapses into the other.

## Selection and routing
Routing produces candidates or recommendations. Appointment requires authorized human or governed business acceptance. Platform ranking, Trust signals or network availability do not automatically appoint a Provider.

## Outcome and Trust
Returned outcomes may update organization-specific or network-level Trust only under disclosed provenance and governance. Trust feedback does not transfer ownership of private relationship history or create a universal professional score.

## Non-goals
No Product specification edits, implementation work, automatic Provider appointment, payment custody, production deployment or public distribution.
