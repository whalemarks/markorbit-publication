## Workstreams

- separate Content, Artifact and Document identity;
- separate Render from material Edit;

- separate Delivery and Publish from formal-state mutation;

### 2. Network participation and Trust — CH33–CH36

- distinguish private participant relationships from platform-owned network supply;
- distinguish network visibility from access;
- distinguish Routing from human selection and appointment;
- keep Trust evidence contextual, scoped, provenance-bearing and challengeable;
- preserve negative evidence and prevent silent score cleansing.

### 3. Cross-Workplace governance and portability — CH37–CH38

- preserve Originating and Execution Provider Workplace sovereignty;
- require typed, bounded and accepted Handoff/Return exchanges;
- prevent customer-relationship transfer by collaboration alone;
- define portable, conditionally portable and non-portable assets;
- define revocation, exit, retention and audit continuity.

### 4. Full-book conclusion — CH39

- restate the complete responsibility chain without granting implementation authority;
- connect Workplace sovereignty, Product focus, governed Execution, formal-state authority and network participation;
- preserve explicit Owner acceptance, freeze and publication gates.
