## Authority model

```text
Workplace business sovereignty
Core semantic authority
Owning Service formal-state authority
Physical custody
Legal / source authority
```

These dimensions may coexist around the same information. None may be inferred from another.

## Mandatory rules

1. Workplace is Organization-scoped, not a generic platform tenant.
2. Customer relationships, private commercial context, internal Knowledge, approvals and business accountability remain with the Originating Workplace unless an explicit legal arrangement says otherwise.
3. Core controls shared meaning, identifiers, contracts and state vocabulary.
4. Owning Services create or mutate formal business facts.
5. Hosting or infrastructure administration does not create business or legal authority.
6. Official sources and qualified professionals retain their applicable source, evidentiary and professional authority.
7. Platform administrators receive no default right to inspect Partner content.
8. Support access must be explicit, purpose-limited, time-bounded and auditable.
9. AI operates under the same Workplace, user, Product and purpose scope as the initiating context.
10. Cross-Workplace sharing requires a typed Projection, Handoff or Return boundary; WP-B establishes the authority rule but leaves detailed collaboration mechanics to WP-E.
