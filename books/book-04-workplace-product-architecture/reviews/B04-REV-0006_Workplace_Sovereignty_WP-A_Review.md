## Key editorial finding

Book 04 RC1 often treats Workplace as an independent Orbit, operating environment or architecture shell. These descriptions remain useful but are incomplete under vNext.1. The next-version edition must make the stronger controlled definition explicit:

> Workplace is the Organization-scoped business-sovereignty, accountability, permission, Product-operation and projection boundary.

The correction must preserve the independent-Orbit metaphor while preventing it from implying semantic ownership, unrestricted formal-state mutation, ownership of platform networks or unrestricted portability.
