## Architecture Summary

Book 04 defines Workplace as the architecture and platform shell of an independent Organization Orbit. It covers organization identity and context; people and accountability; private Knowledge and authorized AI Context; operating surfaces; data boundaries; consumption of Information, Knowledge, Intelligence, Capability, and Skill; AI placement; recommendations; handoff to governed Execution; Product Architecture; Product embedding and cross-Product continuity; architectural profiles for Lite, MarkReg, and MGSN Gateway; Asset, Content, Artifact, Document, Render, Edit, Delivery, Publish, Formalization, outcome, and feedback boundaries; network participation; Routing; Capability Evidence; Trust; and ecosystem learning without centralization.
