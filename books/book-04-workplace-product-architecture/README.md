## vNext Controlled Definition

> **Workplace is the Organization-scoped business-sovereignty, accountability, permission, Product-operation and projection boundary.**

```text
Workplace business sovereignty
≠ Core semantic authority
≠ Owning Service formal-state authority
≠ physical custody
≠ legal / source authority
```

The next-version correction also reconciles:

- Product Installation, entitlement and configuration;
- Product Projection versus Handoff and Return;
- Lite as a lightweight Workplace Product;
- MarkReg self-operated business through an isolated Workplace;
- Sites as a Workplace public-brand/content projection Product;
- MGSN Connection / Gateway versus platform-owned MGSN Network;
- Originating and Execution Provider Workplaces;
- cross-Workplace permission and professional responsibility;
- governed portability and exclusion of platform-owned network assets;
- AI inheritance of Workplace scope and projection rules.
