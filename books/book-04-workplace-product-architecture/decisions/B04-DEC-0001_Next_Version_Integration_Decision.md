# B04-DEC-0001 — Next-Version Integration Decision

## Decision

```text
GO — authorize preparation of an integrated Book 04 vNext Candidate Baseline.
```

## Basis

The full-book audit records:

- 40 / 40 chapters accounted for;
- 12 / 12 required correction classes covered;
- 4 / 4 amendment packages accepted;
- 0 blocking contradictions;
- 0 RC1 manuscript changes;
- no immediate Book 02 Change Proposal.

## Meaning of GO

GO authorizes a separate controlled integration task to create a new candidate manuscript by combining:

```text
Book 04 RC1
+ accepted WP-B amendment
+ accepted WP-C amendment
+ accepted WP-D amendment
+ accepted WP-E amendment
```

The integration task must preserve chapter-level provenance and supersession records.

## What this decision does not mean

GO does not mean that:

- an integrated candidate already exists;
- Book 04 vNext is owner accepted;
- RC1 is deleted or rewritten;
- the candidate is frozen or publication ready;
- Book 02 is amended;
- implementation or deployment is authorized;
- public or commercial distribution is authorized.
