## 3. Historical Editorial Drift

The following upstream phrases should be considered for a future maintenance errata, without reopening accepted semantics:

- Book 01 and Book 02 historical sequence references to `Book 03 — Workplace Framework`;
- Book 03 planning and explanatory uses of `Book 04 Product System`;
- Book 03 descriptions of Workplace as a `product surface` where the intended meaning is the user-facing surface through which Execution is consumed.

The accepted Book 04 interpretation is:

```text
Workplace may expose Product and operating surfaces.
Workplace itself is not reduced to one Product surface.
Workplace represents the independent Organization Orbit operationally.
```

## 4. No Silent Upstream Change

This reconciliation records interpretation and publication continuity. It does not edit the frozen Book 02 baseline or owner-accepted Book 03 manuscript. Any future upstream wording correction must use the applicable publication governance path.
