# B03-PLN-0009 Communication Boundary

## Purpose

Define communication execution boundaries for Book 03 planning without creating Product UI specifications or expanding AI/agent authority.

## Boundary Definitions

- Draft: prepare proposed communication content for review. AI may draft.
- Review: inspect draft content, supporting evidence, recipients, risks, and policy constraints.
- Approve: authorized human decision that a communication may be sent. AI may not approve.
- Send: transmit communication to an external or protected recipient. Sending is protected execution.
- Record: store the approved content, sender, recipient, timing, evidence, and outcome according to approved Core contracts.
- Audit: inspect records and traces to confirm the communication followed required gates.

## Communication Rules

AI may draft but may not send communications. Agents may assist with preparation, routing, completeness checks, and audit summaries, but may not approve or transmit communications.

Communication sending is protected execution and requires Human Review plus applicable permission and policy gates.

## Scope Limits

Book 03 may describe execution sequencing for draft, review, approve, send, record, and audit. It must not specify Product UI, messaging infrastructure, new communication schemas, or Book 02 Core rules.
