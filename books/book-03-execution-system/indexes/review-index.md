# Appendix E — Review Gate Index

## Review Decision Model

| Decision | Execution meaning |
|---|---|
| Approve | May proceed within exact scope and conditions |
| Approve with conditions | May proceed only after stated conditions are satisfied |
| Reject | Must not proceed in the reviewed form |
| Revision requested | New version required before a new decision |
| Abstain | Reviewer does not decide because of conflict, authority or information limits |
| Escalate | Another eligible authority is required |
| No decision | No approval exists |

## Review Gate Requirements

A valid Human Review identifies:

- exact subject and version;
- intended action;
- eligible reviewer;
- Permission and Policy context;
- decision and time;
- scope and conditions;
- expiry, revocation or supersession where applicable.

## Review Locks

- Validation is not Human Review.
- Acknowledgment is not approval.
- Approval is not authorization for unrelated action.
- Approval is not execution.
- AI and agents cannot satisfy review or quorum.
- Material change makes the prior decision stale.
- Retrospective review does not rewrite history.
