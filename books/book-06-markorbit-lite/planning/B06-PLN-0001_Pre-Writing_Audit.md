## 4. Existing constitutional baseline

### 4.1 Book 01 — business and operating-system principles

Book 01 establishes that:

- MarkOrbit is an operating system, not one centralized business or mega-Product;
- the Organization remains the legal, commercial and professional actor;
- infrastructure creates capability, while independent organizations create customer value;
- Workplace is where people, Knowledge, AI, services, relationships and business rules become daily operation;
- each organization retains its customers, reputation, strategy, pricing, partnerships and growth model;
- professional service creates outcomes, trust and long-term relationships.

**Book 06 consequence:** Lite may help an organization grow, but it cannot reduce customer value, professional judgment, safe operation and relationship continuity to a single revenue metric.

### 4.2 Book 02 — shared meaning and formal ownership

Book 02 owns shared Core semantics and formal object/service boundaries, including:

- Identity, Organization, User, Role, Permission and Policy;
- Customer and Customer Contact;
- Brand, Brand Asset, Trademark and Mark Representation;
- Jurisdiction, Classification and Goods/Services Items;
- Document, Evidence, Order, Matter, Task and Opportunity;
- Communication, Review and responsibility records;
- Capability, AI Agent, Agent Contract, AI Output, AI Recommendation and Structured Context.

`Lead` remains Product-local until separately validated. A formal `Opportunity` is not a lead card or model inference; it is created or rejected through its owning service and review boundary.

**Book 06 consequence:** Lite may create Product-local observations, signals, Value Candidates, recommendations, prepared artifacts and handoff requests. It must not create parallel Customer, Opportunity, Task, Communication, Document, Evidence or Matter semantics.

### 4.3 Book 03 — governed work and protected action

Book 03 establishes:

```text
AI or Product identifies and prepares
→ Human reviews or decides where required
→ Execution validates identity, permission, policy, version and state
→ Owning Service performs and records the allowed operation
```

Important locks include:

```text
Task suggestion ≠ active Task
Communication draft ≠ sent Communication
Prepared Action ≠ authorized action
Human Review ≠ execution authority
Service acceptance ≠ external outcome
```

**Book 06 consequence:** “one-click send,” “automatic follow-up,” “one-click publish,” Task creation, Opportunity creation, payment, provider instruction and official action must be expressed as governed modes and handoffs, not silent Lite side effects.

### 4.4 Book 04 — Lite architectural profile

Book 04 already defines Lite as:

> a Today-centered lightweight Workplace Product for trademark professionals, primarily independent professionals and small teams.

The accepted Product loop is:

```text
Today
→ Understand
→ Choose
→ Prepare
→ Confirm
→ Handoff
→ Outcome
→ Learn
```

The shorter action spine is:

```text
Today
→ Recommendation
→ Prepared Action
→ User Confirmation
→ Product or Workflow Handoff
```

The accepted character is:

```text
individual-first
but organization-centered
```

Lite reduces interface and administration complexity. It does not weaken identity, Permission, Policy, Human Review, source trace, data boundaries or Owning Service authority.

Book 04 also anticipates:

- Today and Next Best Action;
- Assistant, Guide and Personal AI Clone;
- client follow-up and service-conversion candidates;
- content opportunities and business Artifact preparation;
- local and private data participation;
- a local case database and personal professional memory;
- personal versus organization memory;
- MarkReg and MGSN handoffs;
- standalone, embedded, mobile, local and degraded modes;
- metrics for missed-work prevention, accepted Prepared Actions, client-follow-up outcomes, Artifact use, handoff conversion and false positives.

**Book 06 consequence:** Book 06 is not starting from zero. Its task is to turn this architectural profile into a coherent Product constitution, Product-local records, journeys, scenarios, module boundaries, MVP and conformance model.

### 4.5 Book 05 — MarkReg interface boundary

Book 05 freezes the Lite-to-MarkReg relationship as:

```text
Lite local / personal / lightweight-team context
→ typed Handoff Envelope
→ MarkReg context revalidation and Product Session
→ governed MarkReg journey
→ typed Return Envelope
→ Lite attention and outcome presentation
```

Lite does not decide filing readiness, alter legal Rules, create Filing Approval or inherit external-action authority. Local notes and personal context retain their source, scope and visibility.

**Book 06 consequence:** Lite must consume the Book 05 Handoff and Return contracts rather than implement a second filing, examination, renewal or portfolio lifecycle.

## 5. Canonical Book 06 starting position

The current repository supports the following candidate synthesis:

> MarkOrbit Lite is a Today-centered lightweight Workplace Product for trademark professionals. It helps independent professionals and small teams convert fragmented signals, authorized context, governed Knowledge and cross-Product outcomes into explainable recommendations, Prepared Actions and business Artifacts, then hands consequential work to the correct Product, Human Review, Execution boundary or Owning Service.

This is an audit synthesis, not yet an owner-accepted Product Charter.

## 6. Product value model

Book 04 already defines several value dimensions:

```text
Reduce Missed Work
Improve Professional Judgment
Accelerate Service Conversion
Improve Delivery Quality
Accumulate Organizational Capability
```

Additional value includes client trust, risk reduction, response speed, privacy and reduced rework.

Book 06 should therefore treat **growth and service conversion as a major Product outcome**, but not the only constitutional test.

A conforming value rule is:

> Lite should increase the probability of useful, trusted and commercially valuable professional action without weakening client interest, source quality, professional responsibility or formal authority.

## 9. Case Center architectural assessment

A Case Center is a strong Book 06 candidate, but it must be composed rather than treated as one undifferentiated database.

### 9.1 Internal case context

Internal case context may contain or reference:

- Customer, Trademark, Matter, Application and official-event references;
- source Documents and Communications;
- Evidence and review records;
- fee and provider context;
- timelines, decisions, rationale and outcomes;
- personal notes, organization-private lessons and local-only material.

This layer belongs to authorized Workplace, Product, Core and Local Vault boundaries. It is not automatically shareable or publishable.

### 9.2 Case memory and reusable case asset

A completed or useful case may contribute to:

- organizational memory;
- a private lesson candidate;
- an approved anonymized reference case;
- reusable Case Assets or templates;
- Knowledge contribution candidates.

Promotion requires deliberate review, source trace, client/matter restrictions, anonymization, rights and intended-use approval.

### 9.3 External case artifact

A client-facing case page, PDF, presentation, FAQ, social post or video is a distinct Artifact with:

- purpose and audience;
- selected and minimized facts;
- source references;
- anonymization or disclosure basis;
- professional and rights review;
- version and template lineage;
- Delivery or Publish approval;
- correction and withdrawal behavior.

One internal case can produce several derivative Artifacts. Approval for one audience or channel does not automatically approve every derivative.

### 9.4 Practice–Knowledge–Evidence

`Practice → Knowledge → Evidence` is useful as a client-facing persuasion and explanation model. It should not become a replacement object model.

The architecture must continue to distinguish:

```text
case experience ≠ canonical Knowledge
case file ≠ Evidence
Evidence ≠ permission to publish
successful result ≠ universal professional rule
quantity of cases ≠ proof of quality for every purpose
```

## 10. Data and privacy implications

Case, content and growth features must preserve the Book 04 data zones:

- Public Source;
- Shared Governed;
- Organization Private;
- Client Restricted;
- Matter Restricted;
- Personal Private;
- Local Device / Local Vault;
- Trusted Network Exchange.

Derived candidates are not automatically safe merely because raw files were not synchronized.

Minimum requirements for a case-derived or growth-derived candidate include:

- source reference;
- controller and organization;
- subject and client scope;
- purpose;
- classification;
- confidence and freshness;
- transformation history;
- allowed fields and destinations;
- expiry;
- review requirement.

## 11. AI and Persona implications

Lite may provide a personal and continuous Assistant or Persona/AI Clone that learns approved style, preferences and corrections.

It must not inherit:

- professional identity or licence;
- signature;
- client authority;
- approval power;
- external-send authority;
- access to all personal or organization memory;
- permission to publish private cases;
- permission to convert one accepted output into organization policy.

Material AI outputs must preserve source, Agent identity/capability, purpose, data scope, uncertainty, prohibited conclusions and Human disposition.

## 12. Product module implications

The audit supports further Product-definition work around five candidate pillars:

1. **Today and Attention** — explain what deserves attention and why;
2. **Growth and Relationship Value** — surface client follow-up, service-conversion and risk-reduction candidates;
3. **Case, Knowledge and Artifact Enablement** — turn authorized experience into reusable private memory and reviewed business Artifacts;
4. **Prepared Action and Cross-Product Handoff** — move selected value into MarkReg, MGSN, Review, Execution or an Owning Service;
5. **Personal and Organization Continuity** — preserve preferences, private context, local participation, outcome feedback and learning.

These are planning pillars only. They are not yet a final module list or chapter map.

## 13. Preliminary MVP direction

A Book 06 MVP should prove one narrow end-to-end loop rather than expose every proposed module.

Candidate first loop:

```text
authorized signal or local observation
→ Today explanation
→ user inspection and selection
→ one Prepared Action or reviewed Artifact
→ governed handoff or manual confirmed outcome
→ typed result return
→ feedback and suppression/correction
```

A viable pilot may combine:

- one client-follow-up or service-conversion signal class;
- one case-derived client Artifact;
- one MarkReg launch handoff;
- manual or review-gated Communication/Publish preparation;
- explicit local/private-data restrictions.

It should stop before silent external send, automatic Opportunity creation, automatic provider appointment or autonomous professional action.

## 14. Findings

### PASS

- Existing Books 01–05 provide a coherent Book 06 baseline.
- Lite’s primary users, Today-centered loop and lightweight Workplace identity are already stable.
- Existing Core, Execution, Workplace and MarkReg boundaries are sufficient to begin Product planning.
- Case Center can be defined without changing Book 02 if it remains Product-local and composes existing boundaries.

### EDITORIAL / PRODUCT REFRAMING REQUIRED

- Growth should be elevated as a major Product-market and service-conversion outcome, not made the only constitutional value.
- “one-click” language must distinguish preparation, confirmation, Review, Execution, Delivery and Publish.
- “global agent center” must be expressed as a Lite surface and MGSN handoff, not a second provider network.
- “trademark asset center” must consume Brand, Trademark, Asset, Artifact, Document and Evidence boundaries rather than merge them.
- “case as sales tool” is valid for external Case Artifacts but does not describe the complete internal-case responsibility.

### CHANGE PROPOSAL REQUIRED

None identified in this audit.

Book 02, Book 03 and Book 04 do not require amendment before Book 06 planning begins.
