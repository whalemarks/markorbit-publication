## 2. Purpose

This Chapter Map projects the accepted Product Charter v0.3 and Product Baseline v0.1 into a readable book structure.

It must not:

- redefine Books 01–05;
- turn the Product into a content subscription or lead-list tool;
- copy Book 04's general Workplace architecture;
- copy Book 05's full trademark lifecycle;
- make controlled `ML-*` records into database tables;
- choose implementation technology;
- authorize manuscript drafting before owner acceptance.

## 3. The seven movements

```text
Product Constitution
→ Daily Operating Model
→ Customer and Service Growth
→ Professional Work Products
→ Cases, Memory and Business Assets
→ MarkOrbit Gateways and Continuity
→ Conformance, Economics and Evolution
```

The sequence answers:

1. Why does Lite exist?
2. How does it organize a professional's day?
3. How does it create relevant customer and service value?
4. How does it produce trustworthy work products?
5. How does it accumulate durable professional capability?
6. How does it connect to the rest of MarkOrbit without absorbing authority?
7. How is value validated, packaged and evolved safely?
