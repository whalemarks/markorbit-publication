## 2. Assessment rule

Each idea is tested against:

1. user and commercial value;
2. consistency with Lite’s accepted lightweight-Workplace identity;
3. Core, Execution, Workplace, MarkReg and MGSN boundaries;
4. privacy, professional responsibility and authority;
5. MVP feasibility and evidence required.

Disposition classes:

- **RETAIN** — suitable for the Book 06 core;
- **RETAIN WITH REFRAME** — valuable but wording or ownership must change;
- **SURFACE / HANDOFF ONLY** — Lite may expose it but another Product or service owns the deeper lifecycle;
- **DEFER** — useful after the first validated loop;
- **REJECT** — conflicts with accepted architecture or Product purpose.

## 3. Overall positioning

### Idea

`Lite = AI Growth Workspace / Growth Center / Sales Enablement Platform`

### Assessment

**RETAIN WITH REFRAME**

`AI Growth Workspace` is a strong external market-facing phrase because it distinguishes Lite from generic CRM, OA and AI chat. It is not sufficient as the constitutional identity of Lite.

The accepted architecture remains:

> Lite is a Today-centered lightweight Workplace Product for trademark professionals.

A compatible Product-market expression is:

> Lite is the AI-assisted growth and action workspace through which trademark professionals discover value, prepare trusted work and move selected opportunities into governed outcomes.

Possible future use:

- external positioning phrase;
- a Growth profile or edition;
- a Product narrative under the canonical Lite identity.

It should not redefine Workplace, Core or the full range of professional value.

## 4. “Lite helps agents close more customers”

### Assessment

**RETAIN WITH REFRAME**

Service conversion is a major reason to use Lite and should be measurable. It cannot become the sole feature-admission rule.

The rejected absolute rule is:

```text
If a feature does not directly produce more deals,
it should not be prioritized.
```

This would under-prioritize:

- missed-deadline prevention;
- professional quality and review;
- client-interest safeguards;
- source verification;
- privacy;
- failure recovery;
- relationship continuity;
- organizational learning.

Recommended rule:

> A Lite capability should improve professional value by reducing missed work, improving judgment, strengthening trust, accelerating appropriate service conversion, improving delivery or accumulating reusable capability. Growth must not override client interest or professional responsibility.

## 5. AI Workbench

### Idea

AI should tell the user what is most worth writing or doing today, rather than waiting for a prompt.

### Assessment

**RETAIN**

This directly matches Today, Recommendation and Next Best Action.

Required flow:

```text
source observation
→ signal
→ Value Candidate
→ explainable recommendation
→ user inspection/selection
→ content or action preparation
→ review/confirmation
→ handoff, Delivery or Publish
→ typed outcome
→ feedback
```

Required controls:

- source and freshness;
- reason for ranking;
- subject and beneficiary;
- confidence and uncertainty;
- duplication/suppression;
- rights and professional review;
- no silent publication.

The defensible Product value is discovery, prioritization and context matching—not text generation alone.

## 6. Trademark Asset Center

### Idea

Transform trademarks into sellable assets through packaging, recommendation pages, videos and sharing.

### Assessment

**RETAIN WITH REFRAME**

The phrase “asset” currently spans several accepted meanings and must not collapse them:

```text
Brand Asset / Trademark
≠ reusable Asset Library item
≠ business Artifact
≠ formal Document
≠ Evidence
≠ commercial transaction asset
```

A Book 06 module may be framed as a **Trademark Growth Asset Surface** or **Trademark Presentation and Activation Center** that:

- consumes Brand and Trademark references;
- prepares recommendation pages, sale/interest pages, portfolios, images and videos as Artifacts;
- records intended audience and commercial purpose;
- preserves ownership, authorization, accuracy and rights;
- hands formal trademark transactions or lifecycle work to the appropriate Product/Owning Service.

The final name should be decided in the Product Charter to avoid conflict with the Book 04 Asset Library meaning.

## 7. Opportunity Center

### Idea

Use fee changes, deadlines, portfolio gaps and client context to recommend who should be contacted and what service may be offered.

### Assessment

**RETAIN WITH FORMALIZATION BOUNDARY**

This is a natural Lite growth loop:

```text
observation
→ commercial signal
→ Value Candidate
→ contact/service recommendation
→ user qualification
→ prepared Communication or Opportunity request
→ governed service result
```

Lite may own:

- signal presentation;
- candidate ranking;
- explanation;
- dismissal/snooze;
- qualification draft;
- prepared follow-up Artifact or Communication;
- request to create a formal Opportunity.

Lite does not own:

- the formal Opportunity lifecycle;
- active Task lifecycle;
- silent client-contact authority;
- revenue attribution as unquestioned truth.

## 8. “One-click group send” and automatic follow-up

### Assessment

**DEFER / GOVERNED MODE ONLY**

A safe first mode is:

```text
candidate recipients
→ user inspection
→ purpose-specific message variants
→ source and attachment check
→ Human Review where required
→ exact recipient confirmation
→ Communication handoff
→ service-confirmed send/delivery result
```

The following are non-conforming defaults:

- AI selects final external recipients without review;
- accepting a recommendation sends messages immediately;
- unknown send outcome triggers automatic retry;
- a user-reported message is shown as delivered;
- one message exposes cross-client information.

Automation may increase later under explicit organization policy, channel scope, approval and execution evidence.

## 9. Global Agent Center

### Idea

Countries, services, quotes, lawyers, capabilities, cases and ordering form a global supply-chain center.

### Assessment

**SURFACE / HANDOFF ONLY**

Lite may provide:

- a trusted-provider shortlist from authorized relationships;
- simple fee/capability comparison;
- provider-related Today items;
- a Capability Need preparation Guide;
- a collaboration handoff;
- returned provider or service outcome summaries.

MGSN must own the deeper network responsibilities:

- capability discovery;
- evidence-based provider evaluation;
- Trust and relationship boundaries;
- Routing context;
- cross-organization disclosure;
- collaboration preparation;
- network outcome and capability evidence.

Lite must not become a second provider marketplace, open bidding system or automatic appointment engine.

## 10. Case Center

### Idea

Use real cases to demonstrate experience, support client communication and generate multiple forms of content.

### Assessment

**RETAIN AS A STRONG PRODUCT MODULE CANDIDATE**

The module should be defined through four linked but distinct layers:

```text
Internal Case Context
→ Case Memory / Lesson Candidate
→ Approved Case Reference Asset
→ External Case Artifact and Publish Package
```

### Internal Case Context

Purpose:

- preserve source, timeline, decisions, communications, documents, evidence, fees, provider inputs and outcomes;
- support current professional work and internal retrieval.

Boundary:

- not automatically publishable;
- not one new universal Case object if Matter and Product references are sufficient;
- personal, client, Matter, organization and local scopes remain separate.

### Case Memory / Lesson Candidate

Purpose:

- preserve rationale, correction, successful/failed patterns and later outcomes;
- help avoid repeated mistakes and support future judgment.

Boundary:

- candidate lesson is not shared Knowledge;
- one case outcome is not a universal rule;
- professional review and scope are required before reuse.

### Approved Case Reference Asset

Purpose:

- reusable anonymized example, timeline, visual component, argument pattern or training reference.

Boundary:

- source, client authority, rights, confidentiality, version, jurisdiction and permitted use remain visible;
- reusable does not mean public.

### External Case Artifact

Purpose:

- client case page;
- PDF;
- presentation;
- FAQ;
- social post;
- article;
- video script or video;
- recommendation or sales page.

Boundary:

- each derivative has its own purpose, audience, version, review and Publish/Delivery state;
- Evidence is referenced and minimized, not copied indiscriminately;
- result claims must be sourced and bounded;
- anonymization must be reviewed rather than assumed.

## 11. Cases as a content source

### Assessment

**RETAIN**

One authorized case may feed multiple Artifacts:

```text
reviewed case narrative
├── client explanation
├── FAQ
├── article
├── social post
├── presentation
├── video script
├── video project
└── recommendation page
```

Required lineage:

- source case and allowed facts;
- Content version;
- Artifact purpose;
- Template/Asset version;
- audience and channel;
- disclosure/anonymization basis;
- professional and rights review;
- final Publish or Delivery evidence.

Approval for an internal training deck does not approve a public video.

## 12. Practice–Knowledge–Evidence

### Assessment

**RETAIN AS A PRESENTATION FRAME, NOT AN OBJECT MODEL**

The frame is commercially useful:

```text
Knowledge
→ what the organization understands

Practice
→ what the organization has actually done

Evidence
→ what can support the stated experience or result
```

It should be accompanied by quality dimensions:

- relevance;
- jurisdiction/service scope;
- recency;
- outcome type;
- professional role;
- source and verification;
- limitations.

A count such as “126 cases” is useful context, but it does not by itself prove current law, success probability or fitness for a different client situation.

## 13. One-click Publish

### Assessment

**RETAIN AS PREPARATION; DEFER SILENT PUBLICATION**

The initial conforming mode is:

```text
approved Case or Content Artifact
→ Publish Package
→ platform/channel preview
→ user confirms final account, audience and representation
→ governed or local publish operation
→ platform/user evidence returned
```

`Publish Package ready` must remain different from `published`.

A local helper or browser component may preserve credentials and fill permitted fields, but final user confirmation is the strong default.

| AI Workbench | retain | Today and Attention pillar |
| Trademark Asset Center | reframe | trademark presentation/activation and Artifact pillar |

The final Product structure may use fewer top-level modules. Module labels should follow the Product loop rather than mirror databases or departments.

## 16. MVP disposition

### Include in first Product-loop validation

- Today signal and recommendation;
- explainable prioritization;
- one client-follow-up/service-conversion candidate type;
- one case-to-client-Artifact path;
- one MarkReg Handoff and Return path;
- manual or review-gated Communication/Publish preparation;
- local/private source restrictions and outcome feedback.

### Defer

- broad autonomous multi-channel publishing;
- automatic group sending;
- complete CRM replacement;
- full provider network and ordering;
- universal case ingestion;
- automatic promotion of case experience into Knowledge;
- organization-wide AI memory without explicit boundaries;
- revenue optimization as a dominant ranking rule.
