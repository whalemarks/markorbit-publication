# Book 06 Governance — MarkOrbit Lite

## 1. Authority

Book 06 consumes, in order:

1. MarkOrbit Orbital Architecture Canon and ADR vNext;
2. Books 01–04 Portfolio Baseline;
3. Book 05 RC1 where MarkReg participates;
4. owner-accepted Book 06 Product Charter and specifications once created.

Owner-supplied research and Product discussions are supplemental inputs. They do not override the accepted publications.

## 2. Book responsibility

Book 06 may define:

- Lite Product purpose, users, problems and non-goals;
- Product-local records and views;
- Today, Recommendation and Prepared Action behavior;
- personal Assistant and Persona experience;
- growth, relationship, case and Artifact journeys;
- MarkReg, MGSN, Review, Execution and Owning Service handoffs;
- Lite-specific failures, metrics, pilots, MVP and conformance.

Book 06 may not redefine:

- Book 02 Core objects, services, status or contracts;
- Book 03 Task, Workflow, Review, approval, Communication or Execution authority;
- Book 04 Workplace, Product, data, Artifact, Delivery, Publish or MGSN boundaries;
- Book 05 MarkReg Product records and lifecycle meaning;
- official law, official status or External Protected Action authority.

## 3. Finding classes

Every cross-book issue must be classified as:

```text
PASS
PRODUCT-LOCAL DECISION
EDITORIAL CORRECTION
UPSTREAM FINDING
CHANGE PROPOSAL REQUIRED
```

A Book 06 file must not silently edit accepted upstream meaning.

## 4. Owner gates

Owner acceptance is required for:

- Product Charter;
- chapter map;
- Product record and Decision map;
- reference journeys;
- conformance scenarios;
- Complete Draft 1;
- Release Candidate;
- final publication.
