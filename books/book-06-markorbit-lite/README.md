## MVP 0

```text
Customer Opportunity-to-Governed-Service Loop
```

MVP 0 requires:

- authorized customer/trademark context;
- a timely, explainable Service-Value Candidate;
- visible source quality and missing information;
- a trustworthy work product;
- final user confirmation before customer action;
- typed response and qualification;
- governed Handoff and destination revalidation;
- returned result and continuity;
- scoped Feedback, Correction or reusable capability;
- zero blocking safety/privacy violations;
- Product and commercial evidence.

```text
content volume alone: insufficient
prospect volume alone: insufficient
```

## Evaluation and conformance

```text
Product value
≠ Product conformance
```

Evaluation covers recurring meaningful use, opportunity relevance, work-product utility, customer/service outcomes, Handoff quality, capability accumulation, safety/privacy and sustainable operation.

Conformance projects all `ML-SCN-01–ML-SCN-24` scenarios.

All BLOCKING scenarios must pass. Usage or revenue cannot offset a zero-tolerance violation. A blocked unsafe action may be successful Product behavior.

## Commercial plans and entitlements

```text
Product identity
→ Product Edition
→ Commercial Plan
→ Entitlement Window
→ Fulfillment Observation
```

RMB 99, recurring/daily content, Prospect Candidate supply, Asset limits, Handoff support levels and Render quotas remain commercial experiments.

They do not define the Product constitution or grant broader access, professional authority, provider appointment, protected-action authority or Review bypass.

Fulfillment requires usable, quality-gated output. Generation activity, failed Render, duplicate prospects or script-only delivery when video is promised do not automatically satisfy an entitlement.
