## Review gate

The Product Charter v0.3 acceptance set is:

```text
B06-PLN-0004 v0.3 — Product Charter
+ B06-PLN-0005 v0.3 — OD-01–OD-10
+ B06-PLN-0006 — Commercial Plan and MVP Experiment Candidate
+ B06-REV-0002 — Base architecture Review
+ B06-REV-0003 — Product Charter v0.3 Review
```

`B06-REV-0003` confirms that the revised Charter:

- defines an enduring AI business operating system rather than a temporary content or lead-supply tool;
- preserves Lite's lightweight Workplace Product identity;
- establishes four connected Product loops;
- treats Today as the daily business cockpit;
- prioritizes existing-customer portfolio value as the first reference journey;
- treats content and prospect supply as capabilities and commercial-plan options;
- makes cases, memory and reusable capability a core Product loop;
- preserves Core, Execution, Workplace, MarkReg, MGSN and Owning-Service authority;
- retains Artifact, Render, Edit and Publish as supporting capabilities;
- classifies early data models as historical evidence rather than current authority;
- recommends Customer Opportunity-to-Governed-Service as MVP 0;
- finds no required upstream Change Proposal;
- is ready for owner acceptance through merge.

Owner merge authorizes the Product record / journey / scenario baseline only.

It does not accept a chapter map or manuscript and does not authorize implementation, production or External Protected Action.
