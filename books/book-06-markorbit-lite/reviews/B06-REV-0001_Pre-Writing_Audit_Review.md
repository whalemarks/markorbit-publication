## 5. Accepted starting interpretation

Book 06 must begin from:

```text
Lite
= Today-centered lightweight Workplace Product
for trademark professionals
```

It is:

- individual-first but organization-centered;
- designed primarily for independent professionals and small teams;
- capable of operating as a lightweight entry for larger Workplaces;
- focused on attention, recommendations, Prepared Actions, business Artifacts and cross-Product handoffs;
- able to support growth and service conversion without reducing professional value to revenue only.

## 6. Supplemental idea result

### Product-market positioning

`AI Growth Workspace` is accepted as a strong candidate market-facing expression or future profile. It is not accepted as a replacement for the canonical lightweight-Workplace identity.

### Growth objective

Growth and appropriate service conversion are accepted as major Product outcomes. A sole “more deals” admission test is rejected because it conflicts with missed-work prevention, professional quality, client interest, privacy, trust and organizational capability.

### Case Center

Case Center is accepted as a strong Product-module candidate subject to separation of:

```text
internal case context
→ organizational memory / lesson candidate
→ approved reusable case asset
→ external case Artifact and Publish Package
```

The Review does not create a new Core Case object and does not authorize publication of client or Matter material.

### Opportunity Center

Lite may discover, explain, rank and prepare commercial candidates. Formal Opportunity creation and lifecycle remain with the Opportunity boundary.

### Global Agent Center

Lite may provide an entry surface, trusted shortlist, comparison preview and Capability Need handoff. MGSN retains network discovery, Trust, Routing and cross-organization collaboration depth.

### External action

One-click language is accepted only for preparation or governed handoff where the consequence remains visible. Silent external send, automatic group messaging, autonomous Publish, automatic provider appointment and protected professional action remain unauthorized.

## 8. Data and privacy result

The Case Center, personalization and growth model can conform if Book 06 preserves:

- personal, organization, client, Matter and local scopes;
- source and provenance;
- purpose limitation;
- approved synchronization;
- anonymization/disclosure review;
- Evidence restrictions;
- Artifact and Publish lineage;
- AI context minimization.

No unrestricted data pooling or learning is accepted.
