## 5. MVP 0 review

CH30 correctly defines MVP 0 as:

```text
Customer Opportunity-to-Governed-Service Loop
```

It requires:

- bounded authorized context;
- timely and explainable Service-Value Candidate;
- visible source quality and missing information;
- useful or meaningfully edited professional work product;
- final user confirmation before consequential customer action;
- typed response states;
- qualification separate from weak activity;
- governed MarkReg or other destination Handoff;
- Return continuity;
- scoped Feedback, Correction or reusable capability candidate;
- zero blocking safety/privacy violations;
- Product and commercial evidence.

It preserves the controlled pass rule:

```text
ML-AC-01–ML-AC-11: mandatory
ML-AC-12: evidence collected
commercial threshold: experiment-specific
```

It explicitly rejects:

- content volume as sufficient proof;
- prospect volume as sufficient proof;
- file generation as sufficient proof;
- clicks as qualified need;
- unqualified Opportunity creation as success;
- unsafe revenue as Product value.

Result: **PASS**.

## 7. Zero-tolerance review

CH31 provides an editorial zero-tolerance projection without creating new controlled IDs.

The zero-tolerance conditions preserve:

- no unauthorized Customer/Matter disclosure;
- no silent Candidate-to-formal-state conversion;
- no autonomous consequential customer contact;
- no required Human Review bypass;
- no false completion status;
- no blind retry after unknown consequential outcome;
- no automatic Personal-to-Organization Memory promotion;
- no unauthorized restricted-case reuse;
- no Lite-controlled provider appointment;
- no unauthorized Local Only upload or remote processing;
- no revenue priority over client interest or professional risk;
- no claim that content or lead volume proves Product success.

These conditions are consistent with ML-SCN-01–24 and Book 06 constitutional locks.

Result: **PASS**.
