## 9. Coverage interpretation

Coverage means the manuscript explains the accepted record and its boundary. It does not mean:

- the record needs its own table;
- all chapters may mutate the record;
- every Product Edition must expose the record directly;
- an implementation may ignore the owning service;
- a reader appendix becomes a Specification.
