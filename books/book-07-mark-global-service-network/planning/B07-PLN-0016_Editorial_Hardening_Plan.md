# B07-PLN-0016 — Book 07 Editorial Hardening Plan

## Objective

Prepare the complete CH00–CH33 manuscript for Release Candidate review without changing the accepted Product constitution, controlled baseline or chapter map.

## Working rules

- hardening may clarify, compress, cross-reference and normalize;
- hardening must not silently change controlled meaning;
- any semantic change to Charter, Baseline or accepted architecture requires a separate controlled proposal;
- no implementation artifacts are authorized;
- one coherent branch per hardening wave.

## Wave EH-A — CH00–CH16

Focus:

- reader promise and product constitution;
- managed-network identity;
- Workplace and authority terminology;
- participant roles and connection lifecycle;
- supply formation and procurement;
- transition into Capability Need Projection.

Tasks:

1. normalize titles, headings, punctuation and capitalization;
2. remove unnecessary repetition while retaining safety boundaries;
3. add cross-references to CH02, CH04, CH15, CH16 and CH32 where useful;
4. verify each controlled distinction is introduced before later use;
5. improve Part transitions;
6. confirm no open-marketplace or customer-appropriation language appears.

## Wave EH-B — CH17–CH33

Focus:

- R1–R3 routes;
- routing and bounded choice;
- funds, fulfillment and Return;
- relationship, Trust and governance;
- MVP 0 and implementation boundaries;
- global capability flywheel.

Tasks:

1. normalize route and decision terminology;
2. compress repeated “not equal” formulas where a stable cross-reference is clearer;
3. verify incidents, replacement, disputes and appeals form one coherent escalation chain;
4. normalize Evidence, observation, Return and formal-state language;
5. align CH30–CH33 with the 16 MVP Acceptance Criteria;
6. strengthen the final synthesis without converting strategy into implementation authority.

## Cross-wave editorial checks

- use `Provider` only for the contextual supply role or clearly defined network record;
- use `Organization` for the stable participant;
- distinguish `MGSN Connection / Gateway` from `MGSN Network`;
- distinguish `Originating Workplace` from `Execution Provider Workplace`;
- preserve capitalization of controlled terms consistently;
- keep `Trust` multidimensional and scoped;
- keep `Evidence` source-aware;
- keep R1 external responsibility limitations explicit;
- keep funds and legal prerequisites visible.
