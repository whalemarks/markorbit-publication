## Owner Direction as Product Authority Input

The following are owner-confirmed planning constraints for this audit:

```text
MGSN resources belong to and are governed by the platform.

Demand-side users and providers connect to MGSN,
not to each other as an independent network.

Private space means each participant's own
Workplace, Lite or provider-side boundary.

Users may retain an External Self-Managed Route.

External routes require manual evidence return
and do not receive implied MGSN guarantees.

When no suitable external route exists,
platform matching is the normal default.

The platform may preselect a Recommended Best Route.

The user retains final confirmation
unless a future accepted mandate says otherwise.
```

These directions govern the Pre-Writing Audit and Charter preparation.

They do not yet establish final controlled Product records or implementation contracts.


A future proposal may require upstream review if it attempts to:

- make MGSN the owner of every user customer;
- duplicate Order, Matter, Task, Workflow or Execution;
