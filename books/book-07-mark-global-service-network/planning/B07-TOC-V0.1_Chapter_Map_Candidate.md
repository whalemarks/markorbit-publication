## Book Title

**Book 07 — Mark Global Service Network**

Candidate subtitle:

> Platform-Owned Global Capability, Procurement, Routing and Fulfillment Across Independent Workplaces

## Reader Promise

This book explains how MGSN turns fragmented international professional supply into a platform-managed global capability network while preserving Workplace business sovereignty, provider professional responsibility, bounded user choice, controlled funds and governed fulfillment.
