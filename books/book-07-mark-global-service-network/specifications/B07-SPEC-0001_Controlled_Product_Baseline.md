## 2. Product Constitution

MGSN is the platform-owned and platform-governed managed global professional-service network through which participating Workplaces gain reliable global service capability.

```text
Originating Workplace
→ authorized demand projection
→ MGSN Connection / Gateway
→ platform-owned MGSN Network
→ controlled provider allocation / instruction
→ Execution Provider Workplace
→ governed Return
→ Originating Workplace
```

The Product preserves:

- Workplace business sovereignty;
- Core semantic authority;
- Owning Service formal-state authority;
- provider professional responsibility;
- platform control of network supply, procurement, matching, funds, fulfillment and Trust;
- user final confirmation for ordinary MVP managed routes;
- external self-managed routes with explicit visibility limits.

## 5. Route Constitution

### R1 — External Self-Managed Route

The user may continue using an external provider outside MGSN.

MGSN does not imply provider verification, procurement advantage, funds protection, fulfillment monitoring, replacement or official-status certainty.

The Product must expose the resulting visibility gap and support manual Evidence upload and source-qualified continuity.

### R2 — MGSN Recommended Managed Route

The default managed route when the user lacks a suitable external route or chooses platform capability.

```text
Automatic matching ≠ automatic appointment
User confirmation ≠ Provider Acceptance
Provider Acceptance ≠ completion
```

### R3 — MGSN Managed Preferred-Provider Route

A user may request an admitted preferred provider, subject to Capability, qualification, conflict, availability, procurement, package and risk gates.

## 6. Participant and Role Model

Organization is the stable participant identity.

```text
Organization Participant
├── Demand Role
└── Supply Role
```

Demand and Supply roles are contextual and may coexist.

Dual-role participation does not create peer-to-peer network authority, mixed permissions or automatic financial offset.

## 7. Multi-Provider Supply Portfolio

MGSN should normally maintain multiple qualified providers for a jurisdiction and service.

Candidate portfolio roles:

```text
Recommended Primary
Qualified Alternative
Specialist
Urgent Capacity
Relationship-Preserved
Backup
Pilot / Testing
Restricted / Suspended
```

These are dynamic platform operating classifications, not permanent public ranks.

## 8. User Choice and Disclosure

Default ordinary presentation:

```text
one Recommended Route
+ up to two materially differentiated alternatives
+ reasons, constraints and user price
+ rematch / preferred-provider / R1 options where permitted
```

Provider identity is disclosed progressively when required for professional choice, conflict, responsibility or law. Direct contact details, provider procurement cost, complete supply pools and routing weights remain controlled.

## 9. Bidirectional Introduction

Demand-side and provider-side participants may introduce existing counterparties.

Introduction:

```text
≠ automatic admission
≠ automatic verification
≠ permanent exclusivity
≠ ownership transfer
≠ platform bypass right
```

A verified introduction may create Relationship Provenance, a time-bounded attribution period and a limited incentive after real platform value is completed.

## 10. Commercial and Funds Boundary

The initial Product direction supports platform wholesale and managed-service packages.

```text
provider procurement cost
+ official fees / disbursements
+ platform service, support and risk layer
+ demand-side margin where permitted
= customer-facing offer
```

The Product requires typed funds controls but does not claim a universal escrow, trust-account, payment-institution or client-money status.

Legal entity, funds holder, accounting, tax, foreign-exchange, sanctions and professional client-money rules remain implementation prerequisites.

## 11. Formal-State Boundary

MGSN must not silently duplicate or own:

- Customer;
- Contact;
- Opportunity;
- Order;
- Matter;
- Task;
- Workflow;
- Review;
- Communication send truth;
- Finance ledger or accounting truth;
- MarkReg official lifecycle;
- official outcome.

MGSN may hold authorized projections, recommendations, network engagements, fulfillment observations, Evidence references and Return envelopes.

## 12. MVP 0

MVP 0 is operator-assisted and begins with:

- Chinese trademark agencies and independent professionals;
- existing platform foreign associates and selected verified providers;
- international trademark filing and selected high-volume services;
- typed offers, matching, funds checkpoints, fulfillment monitoring and Return;
- human admission, procurement, complex review, replacement, dispute and suspension.
