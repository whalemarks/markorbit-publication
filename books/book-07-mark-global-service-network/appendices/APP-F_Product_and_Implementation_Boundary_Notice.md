# APP-F — Product and Implementation Boundary Notice

## What Book 07 establishes

Book 07 defines the controlled Product meaning of Mark Global Service Network. It establishes:

- the platform-owned managed-network constitution;
- Workplace business sovereignty;
- participant, supply, procurement and routing responsibilities;
- R1, R2 and R3;
- bounded Recommendation and human choice;
- funds, fulfillment and Return semantics;
- Relationship Provenance, Trust and governance;
- operator-assisted MVP expectations;
- controlled records, journeys, scenarios, contracts and acceptance criteria.

## What publication acceptance does not authorize

Acceptance of Book 07 does not itself authorize:

- production provider admission or allocation;
- sending instructions or external communications;
- filing, payment, signing or any official act;
- payment custody, client-money handling or escrow representation;
- automatic or manual funds release without separate legal and financial authority;
- automatic Provider appointment;
- automated Trust sanctions, Suspension or Retirement;
- formal-state mutation outside the responsible Owning Service;
- unrestricted access across Workplaces;
- unrestricted provider-contact or provider-pool export;
- production deployment or External Protected Action.

## Required authority chain

A future implementation must inherit and preserve:

```text
Active Architecture Canon
→ Core semantic authority
→ Execution protected-action controls
→ Workplace and Product authority
→ Book 07 Product semantics
→ implementation specifications and ADRs
→ legal, financial, security and operational approval
→ tested production deployment
```

Book 07 may require a control without asserting that the legal entity, technical service, regulated permission or production process already exists.

## Product-local records are implementation-neutral

The 56 `MG-*` records are controlled Product meanings.

```text
Product-local record
≠ Core object
≠ database table
≠ API endpoint
≠ Event contract
≠ deployed workflow
```

A future implementation may map one Product-local meaning to several technical objects, or several meanings to a governed aggregate, provided the controlled distinctions remain traceable.

## Funds boundary

MGSN may require Funds Requirements, purpose allocation, Release Conditions and settlement projections. It does not thereby become:

- the authoritative Finance ledger;
- a bank or payment institution;
- an escrow agent;
- a licensed client-money holder;
- an entity authorized to move funds in every jurisdiction.

The applicable operating entity, payment route, custody model, accounting treatment, tax treatment, foreign-exchange controls and release authority must be established separately.

## Provider and professional boundary

Recommendation, User Disposition, Provider Allocation and Provider Acceptance remain distinct. Provider Acceptance does not transfer professional responsibility to MGSN, and MGSN coordination does not replace local professional judgment.

```text
Recommendation ≠ appointment
Allocation ≠ acceptance
Acceptance ≠ completion
Completion report ≠ official truth
```

## Formal-state boundary

MGSN may receive Evidence and a typed Return. Only the authorized Owning Service may apply a formal-state transition after validating source, authority, structure and consistency.

```text
Return received
≠ formal-state mutation
```

## Trust and governance boundary

Trust is a scoped Product input, not a universal public score or automatic sanction authority. Material adverse actions require defined authority, Evidence, proportionality, correction rights, review and Appeal.

## Minimum implementation prerequisites

Before MVP 0 operates on real external matters, the project must establish at least:

1. selected jurisdictions and service packages;
2. admitted pilot providers and authorized Workplaces;
3. operator roles, escalation and Audit;
4. legal and financial operating model;
5. protected-action approvals;
6. Finance integration or controlled manual authority;
7. Evidence and Return requirements;
8. Incident, Replacement and Dispute processes;
9. privacy, security and data-transfer controls;
10. conformance coverage for `MG-SCN-01–32` and `MG-AC-01–16`.

## Reader rule

Treat every projection, recommendation, observation, Return and Trust assessment according to its source and authority. Product clarity is not permission to bypass implementation, legal or professional gates.
