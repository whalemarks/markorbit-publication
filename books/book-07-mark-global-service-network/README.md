## Working Product Interpretation

Mark Global Service Network, or MGSN, is the platform-owned and platform-governed global professional service network through which participating users and Organizations obtain capabilities they do not independently possess.

Its working value proposition is:

> MGSN gives professional users governed access to global service capability, purchasing leverage, controlled funds, reliable fulfillment and continuously improving provider coverage through one stable platform interface.

## Current Constitutional Candidate

```text
Platform-owned global service resources
+ governed provider participation
+ unified capability and service taxonomy

+ controlled routing and interface access
+ funds and settlement control
+ fulfillment and risk governance
+ user need, client context and bounded choice
+ outcome and provider evolution
```

## Critical Balance

MGSN is neither an unrestricted public marketplace nor a passive directory.

The platform must retain control over:

- network entry and provider admission;
- capability classification and verification;
- procurement strategy and negotiated supply terms;
- routing interfaces and disclosure stages;
- funds custody, release and settlement controls;
- fulfillment monitoring, exceptions and suspension;
- network data, aggregate evidence and platform trust signals;
- service continuity and system-wide risk controls.

Users retain bounded authority over:

- customer relationship and customer communication, unless a specific managed-service model says otherwise;
- expression of service need and constraints;
- review of permitted options or platform recommendations;
- required approvals and instructions;
- choice among options where the Product and service model permit choice;
- acceptance of customer-facing commercial terms;
- review of outcomes and correction requests.

Bounded user authority does not mean ownership of MGSN resources, unrestricted provider access, direct extraction of platform supply, or bypass of platform procurement, funds and fulfillment controls.

## Non-Goals

Book 07 must not reduce MGSN to:

```text
provider directory
open bidding hall
free public marketplace
lowest-price procurement board
user-owned partner database
uncontrolled direct-contact network
automatic provider appointment engine
unregulated escrow or payment wallet
single universal provider score
```
