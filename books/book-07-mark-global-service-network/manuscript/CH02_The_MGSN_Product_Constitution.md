# B07-CH-02 — The MGSN Product Constitution

MGSN is the platform-owned and platform-governed managed global professional service network of MarkOrbit.

Its purpose is to turn fragmented professional supply into usable global capability while preserving the independent business sovereignty of participant Workplaces and the professional responsibility of execution providers.

## Constitutional topology

```text
Originating Workplace
→ authorized Capability Need Projection
→ MGSN Connection / Gateway
→ platform-owned MGSN Network
→ controlled provider allocation or instruction
→ Execution Provider Workplace
→ governed Return
→ Originating Workplace
```

The Originating Workplace retains the customer relationship, originating commercial context, Matter responsibility, private pricing, internal Knowledge and approvals. The Execution Provider Workplace retains provider-side operations, professional judgment and professional responsibility. Core retains semantic authority. Owning Services retain formal-state authority.

## What the platform owns or governs

MGSN governs:

- participant admission and network status;
- provider supply portfolios;
- Capability Profiles and verification projections;
- procurement and service-package terms;
- matching and routing intelligence;
- bounded recommendation presentation;
- platform funds and fulfillment controls;
- correction, replacement and dispute pathways;
- platform Trust and network operating evidence;
- controlled network interfaces.

Platform governance does not imply ownership of every participant customer, private method, internal cost, Matter or official outcome.

## Three routes

The network constitution supports:

```text
R1 — External Self-Managed Route
R2 — MGSN Recommended Managed Route
R3 — MGSN Managed Preferred-Provider Route
```

R1 preserves a participant’s right to use an external relationship, but no MGSN procurement, funds, replacement or fulfillment guarantee is implied. R2 is the default managed route. R3 preserves a preferred-provider request inside platform admission, qualification, conflict, availability, procurement and risk controls.

## Human choice and controlled automation

MGSN may identify eligible supply, rank routes and explain a recommendation. It does not equate matching with appointment.

```text
automatic matching ≠ automatic provider appointment
user confirmation ≠ Provider Acceptance
Provider Acceptance ≠ completion
```

Ordinary MVP services retain a human confirmation and operator-control layer.

## Non-goals

MGSN is not:

- an open provider directory;
- a public bidding hall;
- a lowest-price auction;
- a peer-to-peer participant graph;
- a user-owned provider network;
- unrestricted provider-contact export;
- a universal public rating score;
- an unregulated escrow wallet;
- a replacement for Core, Execution, Workplace, MarkReg, Lite or Finance.

## Constitutional test

A Product decision conforms only when it simultaneously preserves:

1. Workplace business sovereignty;
2. platform control of MGSN resources and interfaces;
3. provider professional responsibility;
4. Core and Owning Service authority;
5. user confirmation and Provider Acceptance distinctions;
6. evidence-based fulfillment and typed Return;
7. legal and implementation gates for funds and protected actions.

A feature that improves convenience by violating one of these locks is not an acceptable MGSN feature.
