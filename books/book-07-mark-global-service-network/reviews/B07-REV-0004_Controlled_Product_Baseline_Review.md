## Ownership Review

The baseline does not redefine or silently duplicate:

- Core Organization, Customer or Contact;
- formal Opportunity;
- Order or Matter;
- Task or Workflow;
- Review or Approval;
- Communication send state;
- Finance ledger or accounting truth;
- MarkReg official lifecycle;
- official authority or outcome.

MGSN owns or governs Product-local network participation, supply, procurement, routing, managed Engagement, fulfillment observations, Return envelopes, Trust and network-governance records only within its accepted Product boundary.
