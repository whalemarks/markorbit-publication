## Review Questions

### 1. Does CH30 define a bounded operator-assisted MVP rather than an automated global marketplace?

**PASS.**

CH30 limits MVP 0 to a controlled end-to-end network loop, selected participants, selected services and explicit operator responsibilities. Manual controls are represented as governed operations rather than hidden Product behavior.

### 2. Does CH30 preserve human and protected-action authority?

**PASS.**

User confirmation, provider allocation, conflict interpretation, funds release, replacement, adverse Trust decisions, suspension, formal-state mutation and External Protected Actions remain governed human decisions.

### 3. Does CH31 accurately cover the accepted journeys and scenarios?

**PASS.**

CH31 covers all eight Reference Journeys and all thirty-two Conformance Scenarios by controlled group. It distinguishes end-to-end explanatory journeys from implementation-boundary tests.

### 4. Does CH31 require safe failure and uncertainty representation?

**PASS.**

No-candidate, provider refusal, unknown external outcome, failed conflict, incomplete Evidence and prohibited funds release remain representable states rather than being converted into success.

### 5. Does CH32 preserve cross-Product ownership and authority?

**PASS.**

The chapter keeps Core, Workplace, MGSN, MarkReg, Lite, Execution and Finance responsibilities distinct. It does not make MGSN the Finance ledger, Matter Owning Service, payment-license holder or external execution authority.

### 6. Does CH32 identify legal, financial and operational prerequisites without pretending to resolve them?

**PASS.**

The chapter records contractual role, client-money, payment regulation, tax, foreign exchange, sanctions, professional conduct, data protection and other implementation gates as prerequisites rather than accepted legal conclusions.

### 7. Does CH33 describe a governed network effect rather than growth at any cost?

**PASS.**

The chapter distinguishes healthy demand, supply, procurement, fulfillment, Trust, introduction, dual-role and Workplace capability flywheels from vanity metrics, opaque scoring, paid-routing distortion and customer appropriation.

### 8. Does Wave 7 preserve the platform-owned network and Workplace sovereignty balance?

**PASS.**

The platform retains supply, procurement, routing, funds coordination, fulfillment and network Trust governance. Originating and Execution Provider Workplaces retain their private business, professional and formal-state boundaries.

### 9. Does Wave 7 accidentally authorize implementation?

**PASS — NO IMPLEMENTATION AUTHORIZATION CREATED.**

No schema, API, payment custody, automatic funds release, automatic Provider appointment, automated sanction, production deployment or External Protected Action is authorized.
