| Candidate Route Set | PASS | Need-specific and temporary; not a permanent public ranking. |
| Bounded choice | PASS | One Recommended Route plus up to two materially differentiated alternatives. |
| Explainability | PASS | Reasons and uncertainty are visible without exposing the full pool or confidential procurement logic. |
