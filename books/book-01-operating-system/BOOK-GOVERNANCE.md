# Book 01 Governance

## Purpose

Book 01 owns the industry vision and Operating System principles of MarkOrbit.

It may explain constitutional relationships but may not redefine the frozen Book 02 semantic baseline, Book 03 Execution authority, Book 04 Workplace and Product Architecture or focused Product specifications.

## Editorial Change Classes

### Editorial Correction

May clarify wording, repair broken content, update publication references or align historical language without changing shared semantics.

### Architecture Interpretation

May record how Book 01 principles map to the current repository-level Architecture Canon.

### Semantic Change

Any change that would alter Book 02 Core meaning requires the Book 02 Change Proposal process and must not be made through Book 01.

## Stable Principles

- Industry before Product.
- Architecture before features.
- Shared infrastructure with organizational autonomy.
- Organization as the real actor.
- Workplace as the primary organizational operating environment.
- Candidate before canonical.
- Human responsibility before AI authority.
- Private-first connection and Knowledge.
- Evidence and Human Selection before provider instruction.
- Evolution without forced centralization.

## Non-Authorization

Book 01 does not authorize implementation, deployment, external Communication, filing, payment, provider appointment, official recordal or autonomous professional action.
