## Source Treatment

Book 01 is primarily a conceptual publication. Its claims are architectural arguments, not empirical legal or market statistics.

Where future editions add external facts, every claim should identify:

- source;
- date;
- jurisdiction or scope;
- interpretation;
- whether the source is authoritative, secondary or illustrative.
