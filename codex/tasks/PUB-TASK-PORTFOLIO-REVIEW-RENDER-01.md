# PUB-TASK-PORTFOLIO-REVIEW-RENDER-01

## Objective
Render and preflight seven deterministic PDF review editions from `PORTFOLIO-RC-01` without modifying frozen manuscripts.

## Work
- rebuild the seven publication candidates;
- assemble common and book-specific front matter with each manuscript;
- render 6 x 9 inch searchable PDF review editions;
- resolve candidate-local figure and publication links at render time;
- generate SHA-256 and page-count records;
- render page previews and detect empty or abnormally low-content pages;
- verify page size, PDF readability and extractable text;
- preserve unresolved owner metadata and the `NOT GRANTED` release gate.

## Outputs
- seven PDF review editions as a workflow artifact;
- rendered PNG samples;
- machine-readable preflight report;
- human-readable review report;
- validation workflow.
