# PORTFOLIO-DEC-0005 — Visual and Rendering Specification

## Decision

```text
ACCEPT — PORTFOLIO-VISUAL-0001 and PORTFOLIO-RENDER-0001
are the controlled visual and rendering specifications for PORTFOLIO-RC-01.
```

## Effect

- seven-book series identity is locked;
- book identifiers `OS`, `CORE`, `EXEC`, `WORK`, `REG`, `LITE`, and `NET` are locked;
- 6 × 9 in is the base print trim;
- PDF review, PDF print and EPUB 3 profiles are established;
- missing fonts, broken links, missing figures and stale manifests are build failures;
- font binaries remain outside the repository;
- review rendering may proceed.

## Unresolved owner decisions

The following remain outside this decision:

- licensed font-family selection;
- final cover colors and any optional cover artwork;
- publisher and copyright owner;
- ISBN and barcode;
- print provider, paper, binding and color profile;
- public or commercial distribution authorization.

## Authority boundary

```text
Review rendering: AUTHORIZED
Publication and distribution: NOT AUTHORIZED
Frozen manuscript modification: NOT AUTHORIZED
```
