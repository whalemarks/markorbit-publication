# The MarkOrbit Publication Series

The MarkOrbit Publication Series presents a governed architecture for global brand services, from constitutional platform boundaries to execution, workplace participation, regulatory operations and professional service networks.

## Series sequence

1. **MarkOrbit — The Operating System for Global Brand Services**
2. **MarkOrbit Core Specification**
3. **MarkOrbit Execution System**
4. **MarkOrbit Workplace and Product Architecture**
5. **MarkReg**
6. **MarkOrbit Lite**
7. **Mark Global Service Network**

Publication order expresses the conceptual reading sequence. It does not determine software implementation order, deployment authorization or operational authority.

## Series status

The seven books have repository-controlled frozen baselines. Public or commercial distribution requires a separate Owner release decision.
