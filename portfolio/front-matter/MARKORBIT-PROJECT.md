# About MarkOrbit

MarkOrbit is a governed operating architecture for global brand services. It separates constitutional platform rules, formal business records, workplace-controlled collaboration, product participation, execution authority, professional review and external protected actions.

The publication series is designed to make those boundaries explicit across seven connected books. Each book addresses a distinct layer while preserving the principle that information, recommendation, preparation, review, authorization and formal-state mutation are not interchangeable.

MarkOrbit publications describe architecture, governance and operating models. They do not themselves authorize professional action, filing, payment custody, provider appointment, production deployment or any other External Protected Action.
