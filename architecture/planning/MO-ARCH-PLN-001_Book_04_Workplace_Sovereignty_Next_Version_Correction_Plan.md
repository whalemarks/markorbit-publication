4. MarkReg self-operated business as an isolated Workplace;
5. Lite as a lightweight Workplace Product with optional client-facing projections;
6. Sites as a public-brand/content projection Product;
7. MGSN Connection versus platform-owned MGSN Network;
8. Originating Workplace and Execution Provider Workplace;
9. purpose-limited Handoff and typed Return;
10. portability limits and exclusion of platform-owned provider supply;
11. platform administration versus Partner-data access;
12. AI inheritance of Workplace scope and projection rules.
