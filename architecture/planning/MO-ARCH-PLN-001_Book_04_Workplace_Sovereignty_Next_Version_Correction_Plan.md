# MO-ARCH-PLN-001 — Book 04 Workplace Sovereignty Next-Version Correction Plan

## Status

```text
Planning Record
```

## Objective

Prepare a controlled next-version correction for Book 04 without rewriting its accepted RC1 in place.

## Required corrections

The next Book 04 version must add or reconcile:

1. Workplace as the Organization-scoped business-sovereignty and accountability boundary;
2. the distinction among business sovereignty, semantic authority, formal-state authority, physical custody and legal/source authority;
3. Product Installation, entitlement, configuration and projection scope;
4. MarkReg self-operated business as an isolated Workplace;
5. Lite as a lightweight Workplace Product with optional client-facing projections;
6. Sites as a public-brand/content projection Product;
7. MGSN Connection versus platform-owned MGSN Network;
8. Originating Workplace and Execution Provider Workplace;
9. purpose-limited Handoff and typed Return;
10. portability limits and exclusion of platform-owned provider supply;
11. platform administration versus Partner-data access;
12. AI inheritance of Workplace scope and projection rules.

## Recommended work packages

```text
WP-A — Canon and terminology reconciliation
WP-B — Workplace authority and data-boundary correction
WP-C — Product Installation and projection model
WP-D — MarkReg / Lite / Sites / MGSN interface correction
WP-E — cross-Workplace collaboration and portability
WP-F — full-book impact review and next-version decision
```

## Change-control rule

Book 04 RC1 remains immutable. The correction must produce either:

- a controlled Book 04 vNext amendment edition; or
- a new candidate baseline explicitly superseding affected RC1 interpretations.

## Not authorized

This plan does not authorize manuscript edits, a new release candidate, schema/API changes or implementation.
