## Canonical Meaning of MarkOrbit

- `Mark` refers to trademarks, brands, brand assets and global professional services surrounding them.
- `Orbit` means that every professional service organization has its own independent operating orbit.
- Each organization retains its own clients, data, knowledge, rules, pricing, relationships, brand and commercial autonomy.
- Organizations are connected through capability, service need, knowledge, governed execution, collaboration outcomes and trust.
- MarkOrbit does not replace independent organizations with a centralized marketplace.
- MarkOrbit provides the common professional operating system required for safe orbital connection, capability routing, collaboration and ecosystem evolution.

## Canonical Statement

MarkOrbit is the operating system for global brand professional services. Every professional service organization operates in its own independent orbit and retains control of its clients, data, knowledge, rules, relationships and commercial autonomy. Organizations follow their own paths while being connected through shared Core semantics, governed Execution, professional capabilities, service needs and trust. MarkOrbit does not replace organizations with a centralized platform; it provides the infrastructure for safe connection, capability routing, professional collaboration and common evolution among independent organizational orbits.

“Federated” may describe a governance characteristic, but it does not replace `Orbital` as the primary architecture language.

### Information & Intelligence Plane

```text
Sources
→ Acquisition
→ Information Engine
→ Structured Information
├→ Distillery → Brain
└→ Value Factory → Mo Intelligence
```

- Mo Data is the logical data estate, not one universal database.
- Mo Crawl is acquisition infrastructure.
- Information Engine produces understood and structured information by normalizing and understanding source material.
- Distillery produces knowledge, rule, capability, skill and workflow-fragment candidates for subsequent professional validation and governance review.
- Professional validation and governance determine whether candidates become approved, experimental or rejected records.
- Brain is the logical knowledge and context plane.
- Value Factory creates derived value candidates.
- Mo Intelligence performs scoring, matching, ranking, recommendation and explanation.

### Capability & Execution Plane

```text
Capability Catalog
→ Skill Registry
→ Agent Profiles
→ Assistant / Guide
→ Workflow
→ Human Review / Approval
→ Owning Service
```

```text
Capability = what the system can do
Skill = a concrete, replaceable and testable implementation
AI Agent = a runtime role operating under identity, permission and context
Assistant = an open and continuous user-facing assistance entry
Guide = structured guidance toward a specific goal
Workflow = governed multi-step, multi-state execution
Owning Service = the authority that changes formal business facts
```

## Workplace and Product Locks

### Workplace

> Workplace is the unified professional operating environment of an organization and the primary operating unit of the MarkOrbit ecosystem.

It carries organization identity, users and roles, permissions, clients and relationships, private knowledge, business rules, service catalog, pricing and preferences, AI context, work and review, product access, partner relationships and organizational experience.

Workplace is architecture and platform shell first. It is not currently a separately marketed mega-product. Each Organization Workplace is an independent Orbit.

### MarkOrbit Lite

```text
Core identity:
a lightweight Workplace for trademark professionals

Primary users:
independent professionals and small teams

Secondary role:
a lightweight entry for larger organizations

User-facing identity:
AI clone / Persona Assistant

Primary home experience:
Today Feed / Next Best Action

Major capabilities:
recommendation, content, lead follow-up,
artifact preparation and cross-product handoff
```

Main interaction line:

```text
Today
→ Recommendation
→ Prepared Action
→ User Confirmation
→ Product or Workflow Handoff
```

Lite is not a smaller MarkReg and is not only a content marketing product.

### MarkReg

> The flagship international trademark domain application and the managed professional-service ecosystem showcase built on MarkOrbit.

MarkReg must be able to operate independently and also be embedded in Lite or an organization Workplace. Its scope is the trademark professional-service lifecycle, not all intellectual property.

Long-term lifecycle:

```text
need recognition
→ country, class and goods/services recommendation
→ search and risk
→ proposal and quote
→ intake
→ order
→ matter
→ document preparation
→ filing preparation
→ office action
→ opposition
→ registration
→ renewal and maintenance
→ change and assignment
→ monitoring and follow-up
```

MarkReg must not be reduced to a traditional case-management system.

### MGSN

> The service-routing, capability and trust network connecting independent Workplaces.

Primary nodes are:

```text
Organization / Workplace / Orbit
```

Verified professionals, service capabilities, jurisdiction coverage and performance evidence attach to those nodes.

Network model:

```text
Private Network first
→ Trusted Extended Network
→ Limited Public Discovery
```

MGSN is not an open bidding marketplace and must not automatically appoint providers.
