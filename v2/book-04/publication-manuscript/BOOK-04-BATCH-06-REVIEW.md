## Structural verdict

Batch 06 establishes the principal operating routes available to a Workplace after Needs, Projection, Engagement, Product access and portability have been defined. It preserves internal delivery as a legitimate first-class route, explains how MarkReg can operate inside another Workplace, separates bounded professional contribution from formal Provider fulfillment and places MGSN behind a Workplace-scoped connection.

## Accepted route distinctions

```text
Internal Delivery ≠ ungoverned work
White-label ≠ silent forwarding
Co-delivery ≠ vague joint responsibility
Contribution ≠ complete professional service
Contributor ≠ Provider Organization
MGSN Connection ≠ MGSN Network
Eligibility ≠ recommendation
Recommendation ≠ appointment
Provider Return ≠ validated formal state
```
