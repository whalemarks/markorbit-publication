## Completed scope

This batch establishes the long-form Book 04 manuscript and completes four foundational chapters:

1. why professional work requires a Workplace rather than a tenant dashboard;
2. the separation of Person, Organization, Workplace and Membership;

4. Product Installation and the multi-Product Workplace.

## Main improvements over the v2 master

The master outline stated the correct architecture but did not yet fully explain:

- why the ordinary tenant model fails in professional collaboration;
- how a Person can represent several Organizations without cross-context leakage;
- what happens when Membership begins or ends;
- how customer relationships differ from data possession;
- how formal records remain owned by Owning Services;
- how Lite, MarkReg, Simulation and network views coexist inside one Workplace;
- how Product-specific roles, connectors and AI policies are governed.

These gaps are now addressed at publication-manuscript depth.
