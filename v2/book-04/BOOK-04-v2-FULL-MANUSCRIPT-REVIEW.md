## Semantic locks preserved

- Workplace remains Organization-scoped.
- Person identity does not authorize cross-Workplace data reuse.
- Core remains semantic authority.
- Owning Services retain formal-state authority.
- Projection does not transfer ownership.
- provider or contributor access does not transfer customer relationships.
- Product Installation does not own every record it displays.
- MGSN Connection remains distinct from the MGSN Network.
- professional qualification remains distinct from platform certification.
- current active Canon and frozen Book 04 remain unchanged.

## Product and technical judgment

The manuscript intentionally makes Workplace more than tenancy, permissions or UI. It becomes the platform unit for:

```text
business sovereignty
+ resources and needs
+ Product operation
+ collaboration roles
+ controlled projection
+ data and AI boundaries
+ organizational learning
```

This is necessary for the Lite commerce, MarkReg white-label, certified contribution and MGSN models to coexist without customer or authority confusion.

## Risks to review

1. At 126 short chapters, the manuscript is structurally complete but requires later editorial consolidation.
2. Some concepts may belong in Book 02 public semantics and require a formal impact review.
3. Compensation, settlement and labor-status details remain intentionally high-level pending jurisdiction-specific design.
4. Personal Workplace eligibility requires legal and tax policy before production launch.
5. Workplace health and matching must remain explainable to avoid opaque platform control.
