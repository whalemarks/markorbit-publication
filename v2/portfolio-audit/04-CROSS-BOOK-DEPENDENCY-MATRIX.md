## Highest-risk dependencies

1. Work Package / Assignment / Contribution / Outcome contract gap between Books 02, 03 and 05.
2. Engagement and engagement-role gap between Books 02, 04, 06 and 07.
3. Production Authorization lifecycle across Books 02, 03, 05 and 06.
4. Need specialization and projection across Books 02, 04, 06 and 07.
5. Relationship Owner and Delivery Owner semantics across all operating Products.
6. Boundary between reviewed Outcome, customer Delivery, provider Return and formal-state mutation.

## Change propagation rule

A change in Book 02 candidate semantics requires explicit impact review in every dependent Book. A Product-level change must not silently propagate upward into Core.
