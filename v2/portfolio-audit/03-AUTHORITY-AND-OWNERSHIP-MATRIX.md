# Authority and Ownership Matrix

| Domain | Semantic authority | Business sovereignty | Formal-state authority | Physical custody | Legal/professional authority |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Person / Organization / Workplace | Core contracts | relevant Organization / Workplace | identity or Workplace Owning Service | platform or approved service | identity source, organization records, authorized representatives |
| Customer relationship | Core relationship semantics | Relationship Owner’s Workplace | customer/relationship Owning Service | platform may host | customer authorization and applicable law |
| Capability Definition | Core | platform governance | capability registry service | platform | professional restrictions remain external where applicable |
| Capability Certification | candidate Core credential contract | credential subject and issuer contexts | certification service | platform | issuer authority; not legal qualification |
| Production Authorization | candidate Core authorization contract | authorizing production organization | production-governance service | platform | does not bypass professional qualification |
| Matter | Core contract | accountable servicing Workplace | Matter Owning Service | platform/product store | applicable customer and professional authority |
| Order | Core contract | Contracting Party / Relationship Owner per Engagement | commercial Owning Service | platform/product store | contract and consumer law |
| Work Package / Assignment / Contribution | candidate Core contracts | originating and assigned Workplaces under Engagement | Execution or production Owning Service | platform | scoped professional authority where required |
| Outcome | candidate Core contract | Delivery Owner and originating Workplace | delivery Owning Service; formal mutation remains separate | platform | reviewer/professional authority by risk |
| Trademark data | Core references | source/customer rights remain external | Data Owning Service for accepted data product | data infrastructure | official office and evidence sources |
| Knowledge | shared source/provenance contracts | source rights and Workplace-private knowledge | Knowledge Owning Service for reviewed assets | Markdown vault, object storage, indexes | author, regulator, office, professional source |
| Brain result | typed result contract | requesting Workplace context | Brain service owns result record only | indexes/model runtime | none by inference alone |
| Listing | Lite-local Projection | authorized inventory Workplace | Lite commerce service | platform | owner/resale authorization and marketplace law |
| Engagement | candidate shared contract | participating Workplaces retain their domains | engagement Owning Service | platform | referenced contracts and professional roles |
| MGSN provider record | Core Organization references plus MGSN profile | Provider Organization | MGSN network service | platform | verified local qualification |
| Provider fulfillment | Handoff/Return contracts | originating and provider Workplaces within Engagement | provider and originating Owning Services for their records | both sides/platform | Provider Organization and named qualified professional |
| Payment / settlement | neutral Core references | contracting and receiving parties | payment/settlement Owning Service | payment provider/platform | financial and tax rules |

## Mandatory rules

1. Physical custody never proves business ownership.
2. Core admission never makes Core the formal-state owner.
3. Workplace sovereignty never permits a Workplace to redefine shared semantics.
4. Execution may coordinate a mutation but cannot perform it without the Owning Service.
5. Relationship ownership must be explicit in multi-party engagements.
6. Professional Authority must be explicit for reserved actions.
7. AI and Brain have no independent legal or professional authority.
8. Provider access is engagement-scoped and does not create unrestricted customer rights.
