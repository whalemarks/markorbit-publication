## Purpose

This directory rebuilds Books 01–07 from a ten-year product, technology, network and investment perspective.

The rewrite preserves the strongest accepted foundations—Orbital architecture, Workplace sovereignty, Core semantic authority, Owning Service authority, governed Execution, Product Installation, Handoff / Return and managed MGSN—while correcting the strategic drift that appeared during the first seven-book cycle.

## Canonical strategic thesis

> MarkOrbit is a capability-centered operating system for global brand professional services. It combines trusted data, governed knowledge, AI reasoning, professional capability, Workplace sovereignty, execution governance, products, certified contributors and managed global service networks.

```text
Data gives facts.
Knowledge gives understanding.
Brain produces intelligence.
Capability turns intelligence into professional competence.
Execution turns competence into governed work.
Workplace preserves business sovereignty and accountability.
Products deliver outcomes.
Networks extend lawful and professional fulfillment.
Evidence improves the whole system.
```

## Ten-year design principles

1. **Capability before feature.** Products may change; professional capabilities remain the durable platform unit.
2. **Workplace before marketplace.** Each participant keeps business sovereignty; collaboration occurs through controlled projection and engagement.
3. **Evidence before automation.** AI output, human opinion and network returns remain candidates until validated.
4. **Learning before labor extraction.** The platform improves professionals through training, simulation, certification and paid real work.
5. **Contribution before crowdsourcing.** Human work is structured, qualified, reviewed, compensated and attributable.
6. **Transaction before content-only growth.** Lite must connect content, trademark assets, demand matching and commerce.
7. **Managed network before open bidding.** Professional services are routed by qualification, trust, risk and capacity—not lowest price alone.
8. **Interoperability before monolith.** Core, Execution, Data, Knowledge, Brain, Capability and Products remain separable but contractually connected.
9. **Rights before reuse.** Data, cases, knowledge, customer relationships and professional contributions retain explicit source, permission and usage boundaries.
10. **Ten-year compounding.** Every completed task should improve data quality, knowledge, capability evidence, matching, training or trust.
