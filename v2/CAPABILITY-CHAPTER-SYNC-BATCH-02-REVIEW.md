## Main synchronization results

1. Capability Profile is now explicitly person-held and privacy-governed rather than an employer-owned or universal platform score.
2. Capability Twin / Agent Capability Twin is defined as a contextual operating projection, not a personality replica or autonomous assignment authority.
3. Capability Ledger records evidence-bearing history without creating Professional Qualification, Eligibility or authority.
4. Reflection Candidate is separated from raw conversation, manager-visible performance records, shared Knowledge and canonical Principle.
5. AI Mentor is defined as guidance and reflection support, not Assessment, Certification or Professional Authority.
6. Lite learning-in-work now includes Context Compiler, bounded Capability Composition, Capability Budget and Session Receipt.
7. Source, Composite, Experimental and Verified MO Capability classes are carried into Lite learning and Simulation.
8. MarkReg now distinguishes Official Fact, Service Outcome, Execution Evidence, user Capability evidence and Provider Supply Capability evidence.
9. MarkReg Outcome and Case evidence may support Capability Evaluation and a Capability Change Proposal, but no Product directly mutates shared Capability canon.
10. Paid task completion and accepted Contributions remain contextual Evidence rather than automatic Capability verification.

## Product boundaries preserved

- Workplace holds business context, authorized Capability projections and private organizational learning.
- MarkReg remains the full-lifecycle trademark service Product and a Capability proving ground, not the owner of shared Capability definitions.
- Lite remains the daily Capability Workplace for trademark professionals and small teams.
- Execution remains responsible for runtime request, composition, Assignment, Review, Approval and Apply boundaries.
- Core and Capability governance remain responsible for shared semantic and Capability change admission.
- MGSN Provider Supply Capability remains distinct from a user's Capability Profile.
