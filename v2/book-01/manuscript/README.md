## Purpose

Book 01 explains why MarkOrbit should exist, what problem it solves, how its architecture differs from ordinary software, and why capability—not feature, order, firm, model or marketplace listing—is the durable unit of the platform.

It is written for five audiences at once:

1. the founder deciding where to invest ten years;
2. the product and technical team translating vision into systems;
3. trademark professionals deciding whether MarkOrbit strengthens or threatens their business;
4. customers deciding whether the platform is trustworthy;
5. investors and strategic partners evaluating whether the platform can compound.

## Core thesis

> MarkOrbit is a capability-centered operating system for global brand professional services. It allows independent Workplaces to preserve customer relationships, business sovereignty and professional responsibility while connecting through governed data, knowledge, AI, capabilities, execution, contribution and managed service networks.

## Reading rule

Book 01 defines the strategic and philosophical system. It does not grant implementation authority, professional qualification, payment custody, autonomous filing authority or unrestricted access to another Workplace's data.
