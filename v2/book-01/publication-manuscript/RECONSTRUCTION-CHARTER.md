# Book 01 v2 Publication Reconstruction Charter

## 1. Objective

Reconstruct Book 01 as a serious strategic and architectural book capable of supporting founder decisions, product planning, technical design, professional participation and long-term investment judgment.

The manuscript must do more than describe MarkOrbit. It must explain why the platform is necessary, why simpler alternatives are insufficient, what institutional choices it makes, what risks it creates and how its value can compound over ten years.

## 2. Source layers

The reconstruction draws from four source layers:

```text
Layer A — frozen Book 01 baseline
Layer B — active Orbital Architecture and decision register
Layer C — Books 01–07 v2 architecture manuscripts and portfolio audit
Layer D — current product strategy, operating evidence and unresolved decisions
```

No layer is copied mechanically. Valuable prior material is retained where it remains compatible; conflicting material is explicitly revised or retired.

## 3. What must be preserved

The following strategic locks remain in force:

- MarkOrbit is the operating system for global brand professional services.
- Capability is the durable unit connecting people, AI, tools, products and networks.
- Workplace is the business-sovereignty and Product-operation boundary.
- Core is semantic authority, not the owner of all business state.
- Owning Services retain formal-state authority.
- Execution governs work, review, protected action, failure and evidence.
- Lite, MarkReg and MGSN are distinct products or operating systems with controlled handoffs.
- The Professional Contribution Network is distinct from MGSN formal service fulfillment.
- Customer relationships do not transfer merely because another party contributes or fulfills work.
- Certification, Production Authorization and Professional Qualification remain distinct.
- AI recommendations and provider Returns do not silently become formal truth.

## 4. What must be corrected

The new manuscript must avoid recurring weaknesses in prior drafts:

- excessive chapter fragmentation;
- definitions without sustained argument;
- architecture diagrams without commercial consequences;
- visionary claims without operating mechanisms;
- software language that obscures professional responsibility;
- repeated slogans replacing evidence;
- treating all participants as identical users;
- presenting network growth as automatically beneficial;
- assuming AI capability equals legal or professional authority;
- treating publication volume as completion.

## 5. Chapter acceptance standard

A chapter is accepted only when it contains most of the following:

1. a concrete industry problem or strategic question;
2. a causal explanation rather than a list of symptoms;
3. a proposed MarkOrbit principle or mechanism;
4. clear distinction from adjacent concepts;
5. at least one realistic operating example;
6. failure modes or counterarguments;
7. implications for product and architecture;
8. implications for business model or governance;
9. a transition into the next chapter;
10. no silent conflict with active authority.

## 6. Target scale

The final Book 01 should likely contain 28–36 substantial chapters in seven parts.

A normal chapter should be long enough to develop one full argument. Some chapters may be 2,000–4,000 words; foundational chapters may be longer. Word count remains a diagnostic, not the acceptance criterion.

## 7. Reconstruction sequence

```text
Batch 01 — industry structure and missing operating layer
Batch 02 — why capability is the durable unit
Batch 03 — Data, Knowledge, Brain and Execution
Batch 04 — Workplace sovereignty and collaboration
Batch 05 — Lite, MarkReg, MGSN and the product portfolio
Batch 06 — economics, trust, governance and professional development
Batch 07 — ten-year roadmap, risks, investment thesis and closing
Batch 08 — editorial integration, duplication removal, figures and release review
```

## 8. Evidence policy

Book 01 is a strategic architecture book, not a legal treatise. It may use generalized industry examples and clearly labeled hypothetical scenarios.

Specific legal rules, market statistics or named external cases require sourced verification before publication. Unsupported numerical claims must not be introduced merely to make the argument appear stronger.

## 9. Publication status

Merging reconstruction batches means the working manuscript is accepted for continued development. It does not freeze the manuscript, replace the active Book 01 baseline or authorize public commercial publication.
