## Why this directory exists

The earlier `v2/book-01/manuscript/` material established the strategic architecture, but many chapters remained short architecture notes rather than complete publication chapters.

This directory begins the real reconstruction of Book 01. Each chapter must provide sustained argument, examples, counterarguments, architecture implications, commercial consequences and implementation constraints. Chapter count is no longer treated as evidence of completeness.

## Reconstruction standard

A completed chapter should normally answer:

```text
What real problem is being examined?
Why do existing approaches fail?
What does MarkOrbit propose?
How is the concept different from adjacent concepts?
How does it operate in practice?
What can go wrong?
What does it require from products and technology?
What does it change commercially?
What remains unresolved?
```
