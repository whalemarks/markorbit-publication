## Material conclusions

1. Product is defined as a governed capability arrangement, not a feature bundle.
2. Product Installation connects a Product to a Workplace without transferring business sovereignty.
3. Lite is a daily commercial operating surface, not a reduced MarkReg.
4. Lite's content, asset, demand, customer and service-opportunity functions form one commercial loop.
5. AI brand concepts remain demonstrations rather than legal facts or proof of use.
6. Trademark commerce requires verified inventory, authority, structured demand, explainable matching and transfer evidence.
7. Trademark, Inventory, Resale Authorization, Listing, Request, Inquiry and Transaction Opportunity remain distinct.
8. MarkReg is a full-lifecycle service Product and production proving ground.
9. Matter remains distinct from Order.
10. MarkReg may be flagship without becoming the compulsory commercial center of the platform.
