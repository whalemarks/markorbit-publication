## R0–R4 review

The tier model is proportionate rather than maximalist:

- R0 permits deterministic, low-consequence, reversible control;
- R1 permits standardized production with governed sampling;
- R2 requires complete Review before consequential reliance;
- R3 requires senior or cross-professional Review;
- R4 requires qualified final-authority control.

The chapters explicitly prevent:

- treating every automated action as R0;
- treating every human action as safe;
- using a job title as an R-tier;
- converting assessment performance into R4 authority;
- lowering tiers because of commercial or deadline pressure.

## Professional Authority review

AI is not treated as a professional authority or accountable legal actor.

## Protected-action review

External Protected Actions include official filing, professional communication, provider appointment, protected-data disclosure, funds action, public professional publication and other externally consequential transitions.

Every action requires:

- exact payload identity;
- authority chain;
- current Approval;
- current professional qualification where required;
- idempotency or business-level duplicate controls;
- Evidence Contract;
- Unknown and reconciliation handling;
- Owning Service validation before formal-state mutation.
