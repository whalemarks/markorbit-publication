## Authority review

The batch preserves the following execution authority chain:

```text
Capability Request
→ Mode and Implementation Binding
→ Contribution
→ Typed Review
→ Synthesis
→ Specific Approval
→ Apply
```

No AI mode creates Professional Authority, customer representation, Provider Appointment, funds custody or formal-state ownership.

## Delivery Owner review

The Delivery Owner is defined as the accountable coordinator of accepted scope and delivery integrity. The role may manage decomposition, dependencies, versions, Reviews, customer decisions, Provider Returns, escalation and Outcome assembly.

The chapter explicitly prevents the role from absorbing:

- customer relationship ownership;
- contracting-party status;
- professional qualification;
- every Approval authority;
- Owning Service formal-state authority;
- unlimited emergency authority.

## Human–AI mode review

M0–M5 are defined as Capability-invocation policies rather than user-interface labels:

```text
M0 — Human-only
M1 — AI Reference
M2 — AI Assist
M3 — AI Draft, Human Approve
M4 — AI Execute under Governance
M5 — Hybrid Professional Network
```

Mode selection remains task-, jurisdiction-, risk-, data- and policy-specific. A Product cannot silently redefine these modes.

## AI responsibility review

The batch requires provenance for material AI work, including model, policy, Capability, instructions, input versions, sources, tools, memory context, uncertainty and human disposition.

It preserves accountable human and organizational responsibility:

```text
AI Generated
≠ AI Accountable Party
```

Meaningful human control requires competence, time, source visibility and power to reject.

## Memory and training review

Memory is separated into session, task, Matter, customer-authorized, Workplace and reusable Knowledge contexts. Training use is treated as a separate permission domain.

No operational access, Contribution acceptance or de-identification automatically creates training permission.

## Privacy and customer-boundary review

The chapters preserve purpose limitation and cross-customer separation. Delivery access does not permit marketing, publication, networking or model training. One coherent customer experience does not hide represented actors or transfer customer ownership.

## Commercial and labor review

The chapters do not change Contributor compensation or Provider procurement semantics. Multi-professional synthesis and M5 coordination retain typed Contributions, Appointments, Review obligations and responsibility. Commercial preference cannot silently choose a less safe AI mode.
