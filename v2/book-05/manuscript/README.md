## Product thesis

MarkReg is MarkOrbit's flagship full-lifecycle international trademark product and self-operated service model. It converts fragmented customer instructions, professional capability, official requirements, local-provider work and post-filing events into one accountable lifecycle.

MarkReg must scale delivery without automatically acquiring every partner Workplace's customer relationship, without confusing platform certification with legal qualification, and without reducing complex professional work to ungoverned outsourcing.

## Product boundary

MarkReg may operate as:

- a direct self-operated service;
- a white-label delivery service;
- a co-delivery orchestrator;
- a bounded capability provider;
- a transaction and transfer service;
- a governed route into MGSN.

It is not the mandatory contracting party, Relationship Owner or formal professional authority for every matter it supports.
