
- negative search marketed as availability;
- class-only goods analysis;

- forms completed with the wrong applicant;
- commercial logos substituted for filed marks;
- POA uploads treated as valid originals;
- Jurisdiction Pack rules presented as official law;
- Quote acceptance treated as filing authority;
- Provider directories treated as appointment systems;

- HTTP or email success presented as filing completion;
- blind retry under unknown state;
- Provider emails presented as official events;
- office actions treated as generic tasks;
- customer silence treated as consent or abandonment;
- AI drafts presented as final legal strategy;
- evidence uploads presented as sufficient evidence;
- material goods amendments failing to propagate.

No blocking contradiction with Books 01, 03, 04 or 06 was identified.
