# Book 05 v2 Publication Reconstruction Charter

## 1. Initiative

```text
Initiative:
Books 01–07 Full Manuscript Reconstruction

Current controlled unit:
Book 05 — MarkReg
```

The objective is to create a publication-grade long-form manuscript from the accepted v2 architecture while preserving the frozen Book 05 RC1 and tracing valuable historical material.

## 2. Why reconstruction is necessary

Book 05 currently has three substantial but different forms:

1. a frozen RC1 with forty-eight chapters, specifications, appendices, figures and controlled records;
2. a v2 master with a compressed twenty-five-chapter future architecture;
3. a v2 working expansion with seventy-seven short chapters across six Parts.

The RC1 is the strongest developed historical source but predates later Workplace sovereignty and Product-boundary corrections. The v2 master reflects the newer direction but is an architecture outline rather than a complete publication manuscript. The working expansion adds useful coverage but remains too fragmented and thin for final publication.

The reconstruction must therefore combine maturity with current architecture instead of treating any one source as sufficient by itself.

## 3. Governing hierarchy

When sources conflict, use this hierarchy:

```text
Active Canon and Decision Register
→ accepted cross-book architecture corrections
→ active Book 05 v2 master direction
→ frozen Book 05 RC1 as preserved source
→ v2 working manuscript
→ implementation examples and open proposals
```

The hierarchy does not permit the reconstruction to rewrite the Canon. It determines which wording must be revised inside the Book 05 manuscript.

## 4. Primary controlled correction

Older Book 05 materials sometimes describe MarkReg as simultaneously a Product Installation and a self-operated Workplace.

The current architecture requires a sharper distinction:

```text
MarkReg
= Product and service operating model

MarkReg Product Installation
= MarkReg enabled inside a Workplace

MarkReg-operated Workplace
= a self-operated business organization using MarkReg
```

The self-operated Workplace may be Relationship Owner, Contracting Party, Payment Receiver and Delivery Owner in direct service mode. The MarkReg Product itself is not a Workplace and does not automatically hold those roles.

This correction preserves the commercial intent of the old material while aligning it with Workplace sovereignty.

## 5. Reconstruction principles

### Preserve before replacing

Valuable RC1 material should be retained, revised, merged or explicitly deprecated. It should not disappear merely because the chapter structure changes.

### Consolidate without flattening

Short chapters may be merged into longer publication chapters. Distinct state, authority and evidence boundaries must survive consolidation.

### Product continuity without authority collapse

MarkReg may present one accountable lifecycle while preserving:

- Relationship Owner;
- Contracting Party;
- Payment Receiver;
- Delivery Owner;
- Professional Authority;
- Owning Service;
- Provider responsibility;
- official-source authority.

### Need before form

The Product starts with the business objective and decision problem. Formal forms appear after enough context and choices exist.

### Ask less without hiding uncertainty

Question reduction should come from authorized reuse, bounded inference, progressive clarification and option design—not from omission of material facts.

### Candidate before formal truth

Extracted, inferred, generated and externally reported information remains typed and provisional until accepted for a defined purpose by the appropriate actor or service.

### Prepared action before protected action

MarkReg may prepare an exact action package. Review, approval, permission and external execution remain separate.

### Full lifecycle means continuity, not one status

The Product should preserve relationships across filing, prosecution, registration, maintenance, renewal, recordal, transaction and portfolio work while keeping independent state planes.

### Honest stops are valid outcomes

The Product may conclude that more evidence is needed, a route is unsafe, a Provider is unavailable, a deadline is unknown or the customer should not proceed.

## 6. Chapter maturity standard

A publication-grade chapter should avoid becoming:

- a specification list;
- a feature inventory;
- a glossary entry;
- a sequence of authority disclaimers without narrative;
- an implementation PRD;
- a jurisdictional legal opinion.

It should provide a sustained argument and show how the architecture changes customer experience, professional work, Product design and commercial responsibility.

## 7. Source integration states

Every historical section considered during reconstruction should eventually be classified as:

```text
Retained substantially
Revised for current architecture
Merged into another chapter
Moved to apparatus or example
Superseded by current architecture
Deprecated with reason
Unverified or still missing
```

Batch-level Source Integration Registers may remain incomplete until the full historical inventory is reviewed.

## 8. Authority locks

The reconstruction must not imply:

- automatic creation of Customer, Order, Matter or payment state;
- automatic transfer of a customer relationship;
- automatic Provider eligibility, selection, appointment or acceptance;
- AI professional authority;
- autonomous filing, response, settlement, abandonment or transaction action;
- Provider Report as Official Truth;
- MarkReg Product state as formal shared state;
- payment custody, escrow or unrestricted netting;
- public marketplace launch;
- unrestricted implementation authority.

## 9. Commercial neutrality requirement

MarkReg may recommend self-operated service, white-label delivery, co-delivery, bounded capability fulfillment, MGSN fulfillment or another governed route.

Where the platform or an affiliated Workplace earns from a recommended route, the commercial interest should be disclosed at the relevant decision point.

```text
Preferred Product route
≠ hidden mandatory route
```

A Workplace retains the right to use its own internal capability or approved external relationships where the engagement and law permit.

## 10. Batch 01 acceptance criteria

Batch 01 is acceptable only if it:

- establishes the publication workspace and source hierarchy;
- reconciles Product versus self-operated Workplace identity;
- explains one accountable lifecycle without one central authority;
- defines direct, white-label, co-delivery and bounded capability modes;
- establishes decision-ready intake before formal data collection;
- preserves relationship and professional-authority roles;
- does not modify frozen Book 05 or active Canon files;
- records source limitations and remaining editorial work.

## 11. Stop conditions

Stop and request a decision if the reconstruction requires:

- changing active Canon meaning;
- changing a frozen Book 05 RC1 file;
- choosing final prices, commissions or custody structure;
- assigning unresolved legal liability among parties;
- declaring a new mandatory commercial route;
- deleting substantial historical content without a traceable disposition;
- resolving a source conflict that materially changes the Product thesis.
