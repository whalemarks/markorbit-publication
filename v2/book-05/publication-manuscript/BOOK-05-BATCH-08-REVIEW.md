## Structural verdict

Batch 08 completes the planned Book 05 long-form chapter set by connecting governed delivery to learning, capability, routing, content, product investment and long-term platform discipline.

The controlling chain is:

```text
Matter and Delivery Outcome
→ Contextual Evidence
→ Rights and Privacy Review

→ Human Review
→ Capability, Routing, Content or Investment Decision
```

No operational record becomes shared knowledge, public content, professional truth or automatic investment authority by itself.

## CH27 review — case distillation

The chapter correctly distinguishes:

```text
Operational Record
≠ Learning Asset
≠ Shared Knowledge
≠ Public Case
≠ AI-training Permission
```

It establishes separate paths for private Workplace lessons, internal quality cases, de-identified patterns, synthetic simulations, assessments, Knowledge candidates and public case studies.

Key controls include:

- purpose-specific rights review;
- confidentiality, privilege and contractual restrictions;
- de-identification beyond name removal;
- synthetic and composite cases where anonymization is insufficient;
- protected provenance and derivative tracking;
- fact, lesson, recommendation and marketing separation;
- contextual Provider and Contributor evidence;
- separate public-case publication gates;
- AI-training purpose and permission controls;
- correction, withdrawal and derivative propagation.

The chapter preserves the principle that delivery access does not create reuse rights.

## CH28 review — Outcome feedback and routing

The chapter establishes that completion, review acceptance, customer acceptance, official Outcome and financial reconciliation remain distinct.

It prevents simplistic attribution by requiring context such as task difficulty, source quality, supervision, reviewer correction, jurisdiction and later official evidence.

A governed routing path is defined:

```text
Work Package Need
→ Mandatory Eligibility Gates
→ Conflict and Authority Check
→ Capacity and Deadline Check
→ Contextual Quality Evidence
→ Route Recommendation
→ Selection and Acceptance
```

Important controls include:

- no single universal score;
- reviewer-quality measurement;
- Provider Return validation before positive evidence;
- scoped and perishable Capability updates;
- proportional response to negative evidence;
- no quality-history conversion into professional authority;
- current Capacity and Readiness;
- commercial-interest disclosure;
- customer and partner relationship constraints;
- process-defect detection rather than repeated individual blame;
- appeals and correction.

## CH29 review — market signals and investment

The chapter correctly treats market data as evidence for investigation rather than automatic truth.

It separates:

```text
Search Visibility
→ Content Engagement
→ Intent Candidate
→ Qualified Need
→ Decision-ready Intake
→ Quote
→ Customer Instruction
→ Order
→ Matter Outcome
```

It also distinguishes existing-customer obligations, partner-owned opportunities, direct demand, former-customer reactivation, informational interest and unsupported demand.

Key controls include:

- channel and language bias;
- demand and supply readiness;
- precise Capability-gap identification;
- high-consequence failure signals;
- Outcome-adjusted unit economics;
- prohibition on manufacturing lifecycle demand;
- explicit investment hypotheses;
- bounded pilots and stop conditions;
- automation readiness beyond volume;
- no direct mutation of professional truth from conversion data;
- commercial-interest disclosure;
- privacy-preserving cross-Workplace aggregation;
- complete-loop investment tests.
