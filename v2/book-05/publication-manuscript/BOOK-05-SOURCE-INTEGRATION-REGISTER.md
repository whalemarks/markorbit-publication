## Material architecture corrections made

### Product and Workplace correction

```text
MarkReg Product
≠ MarkReg-operated Workplace
```

This correction prevents the Product from automatically becoming customer owner, Contracting Party, payment receiver, Delivery Owner or Professional Authority.

### Registration and continuing-right split

The former combined post-registration topic was expanded into:

- CH09 registration verification and Right Baseline;
- CH10 maintenance, renewal, recordal, recovery and re-filing.

### Growth & Conversion addition

SEO, GEO, automatic conversion and content-quality testing were added as a governed Product layer without absorbing Sites, Lite or Knowledge.

## Publication rule

No source is considered fully integrated solely because its subject appears in a reconstructed chapter. Final publication requires evidence that mature detail, exceptions and qualifications have been deliberately retained, revised, merged or deprecated.
