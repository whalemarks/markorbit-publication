## Failure-mode check

The batch directly addresses:

- separate informal processes for employees, AI, freelancers and Providers;
- one generic worker or owner field;
- AI presented as an accountable professional;
- certification badge unlocking live customer data;
- permanent production authorization;
- routing by self-declared title or lowest price;
- Assignment without task acceptance;
- live customer work disguised as training;
- nominal supervision without available review;
- routine-task volume presented as complex-work competence;
- strong producers automatically made Reviewers;
- unlimited unpaid revision;
- contributor access used to solicit customers;
- white-label work concealing required professional identity;
- private Contributions entering training data without permission;
- Contribution Network collapsed into MGSN;
- exit leaving active access or unpaid work unresolved.

No blocking contradiction with Books 01, 03, 04, 06 or 07 was identified.
