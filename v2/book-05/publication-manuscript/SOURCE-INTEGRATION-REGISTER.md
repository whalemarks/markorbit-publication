### B05-CH-04 — Position Between Workplace, Core, Execution, MGSN and Owning Services

Major source arguments:

- MarkReg owns the focused trademark Product journey, not the whole professional system;
- Workplace supplies authorized context;
- Core supplies shared meaning;
- Execution governs coordinated and protected work;
- MGSN supplies network capability;
- Owning Services preserve formal business facts;
- external Providers and offices retain their authority;
- Product-local state, references, snapshots, Projections, candidates and caches must remain distinct;
- Handoff transfers context and request, not unlimited ownership or authority.

Initial disposition:

```text
CH02 Product, Service and Operating Model: retained and revised
CH03 Customer Relationship Modes: retained and expanded
Later external filing and production chapters: reserved
```

Controlled revision:

Older wording that can be read as equating MarkReg with a self-operated Workplace is revised to:

```text
MarkReg Product
≠ MarkReg-operated Workplace
```

The self-operated Workplace is one commercial operating mode using the Product.
