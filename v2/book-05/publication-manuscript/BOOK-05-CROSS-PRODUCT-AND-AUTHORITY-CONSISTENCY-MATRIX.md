# Book 05 — Cross-product and Authority Consistency Matrix

## Product boundary matrix

| Product or layer | Primary responsibility | Does not automatically own |
|---|---|---|
| Core | Shared semantics, identity and governance contracts | Workplace customers, commercial relationships or all Product state |
| Workplace | Business sovereignty, customer relationships, Membership and Product operation | Platform Products, other Workplaces or MGSN Network assets |
| MarkReg | Service discovery, decision, trademark lifecycle delivery, quality, evidence, economics and learning | Every customer relationship, every Provider or official authority |
| Lite | Daily content growth, trademark supply and demand, service opportunities and Capability activity | Full Matter execution or protected professional action |
| Sites | Public and customer-facing publishing, rendering, indexing, schema and page operation | Professional truth, Customer, Order, Matter or Payment state |
| Knowledge / Brain | Sources, versions, provenance, contradictions and governed knowledge objects | Automatic execution, customer instruction or publication authority |
| MGSN | Managed global professional-service routing and Provider infrastructure | Customer relationships, Workplace sovereignty or automatic appointment |
| External Provider | Scoped professional or local service under accepted Engagement | Full Workplace data, customer ownership or unrestricted future work |

## Cross-book consistency

### Book 01

Book 05 is consistent with the platform constellation: Products remain distinct and communicate through governed Handoffs and Projections.

### Book 03

Book 05 relies on Book 03 for Human–AI authority. AI may organize, infer, draft, compare and assist review; it does not become customer, professional, Provider or official authority.

### Book 04

Book 05 preserves Workplace sovereignty, Relationship Owner, partner protection, bounded Contribution, Assignment-scoped access and structured exit.

### Book 06

Lite may originate content, Requests, service opportunities, Inquiries and transaction opportunities. MarkReg receives typed Handoffs for full service delivery. Handoff does not transfer customer relationships or create execution authority.

### Book 07

MGSN may provide Provider discovery and managed service routes. MarkReg still requires selection, appointment, acceptance, Evidence Return and Owning Service validation.

- Knowledge owns source and freshness context;
- Sites owns page publishing and technical operation;
