### 1. Core remains a semantic constitution

```text
Core defines shared meaning.
Core does not own every record.
```

The manuscript rejects a centralized-object or centralized-database interpretation of Core.

### 2. Authority is multi-dimensional

The following remain separate:

```text
business sovereignty
semantic authority
formal-state authority
physical custody
legal, evidentiary and professional authority
```

No generic `owner` field may collapse them.

The manuscript keeps Observation, Candidate, Contribution, Outcome, Provider Return and formal state distinct.

```text
Provider Return
≠ accepted truth

≠ formal-state mutation
```
