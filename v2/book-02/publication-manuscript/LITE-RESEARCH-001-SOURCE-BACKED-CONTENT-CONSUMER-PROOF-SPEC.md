# LITE-RESEARCH-001 — Source-backed Content Consumer Proof Specification

## Objective

Demonstrate whether Lite can consume the Batch 13 noncanonical profiles to produce one source-backed trademark content package without promoting Product-local content records into Core.

## Consumer scenario

```text
source-backed content Need or Opportunity
→ content Work Package profile
→ research and drafting Tasks
→ internal or bilateral Assignment
→ sourced Contributions
→ Human Review
→ publication-readiness Outcome
```

The proof ends before public publication, marketing distribution, paid promotion or Customer creation.

## Required use case

Create one fixture-only content package explaining a trademark topic using:

- at least two sourced Knowledge or Data references;
- one research Task;
- one drafting Task;
- one source-check Task;
- one communication or publication Review Task;
- an AI-assisted draft Contribution;
- a corrected Contribution after Review;
- a purpose-limited publication-readiness Outcome.

## Required profile use

### Production Authorization

Show that an AI implementation may retrieve and draft within declared source, Tool, data and model-version limits.

Required lock:

```text
AI Draft Authorized
≠ Public Publication Approved
≠ Professional Advice Authorized
```

### Work Package

Group source research, drafting, source verification and editorial Review without replacing frozen Tasks.

### Assignment

Use Task assignment for internal work. Use bilateral Assignment only where an external Contributor explicitly accepts scope, confidentiality or delivery obligations.

### Contribution

Preserve:

- source references;
- draft version;
- contributor or model attribution;
- assumptions and Unknowns;
- exact corrections;
- superseded versions.

### Outcome

Represent that the content package is acceptable for a specified channel and audience, subject to a separate Apply or publication decision.

```text
Publication-readiness Outcome
≠ Published
≠ Marketing Consent
≠ Professional Opinion
```

## Product-local records

The following remain Lite-owned:

```text
Content Brief
Audience Profile
Editorial Angle
Campaign Asset
Channel Plan
Publishing Schedule
Content Opportunity
Lead Candidate
Performance Metric
```

Repeated use does not promote them into Core.

## Minimum fixture sequence

```text
WP-LITE-001 research profile created
→ source and drafting Tasks linked
→ bounded AI Production Authorization referenced
→ Contribution v1 generated with citations
→ source Review detects unsupported Claim
→ Contribution v2 removes or qualifies Claim
→ publication-readiness Outcome accepted for one channel
→ no publication Apply occurs
```

## Required negative cases

Reject or flag:

1. public source record treated as Customer consent.
2. Opportunity treated as marketing authorization.
3. AI draft without source or model provenance.
4. retrieved source treated as verified current Claim.
5. unsupported Claim accepted because model confidence is high.
6. all Tasks Completed treated as publication approval.
7. publication-readiness Outcome treated as content already published.
8. customer or Matter data reused as shared content without rights review.
9. operational access treated as AI-training permission.
10. external Contributor Assignment treated as relationship ownership.

## Evidence required

The proof must produce:

- positive and negative fixtures;
- schema and validation results;
- source and Claim trace;
- Contribution correction history;
- Product-local extension list;
- fields shared with MarkReg and Execution;
- fields unique to Lite;
- boundary assertion results;
- recommendation on shared-contract necessity.

## Success criteria

- the content package is representable without new frozen Core Objects;
- Task and Human Review remain canonical and unchanged;
- Production Authorization remains a Permission profile;
- Contribution and Outcome remain profiles;
- Lite content records remain Product-local;
- no public publication or marketing action occurs;
- no Customer, consent or relationship is inferred from public data;
- exact citation and supersession behavior is demonstrated.

## Consumer-level target

```text
L2 fixture-level proof
```

The proof is not a production implementation. It is sufficient for cross-consumer comparison only when fixtures and validators are reproducible.
