## Purpose

Book 06 explains how MarkOrbit Lite can become the daily operating Product for independent trademark professionals and small teams without becoming a reduced MarkReg, a generic content generator, an uncontrolled public marketplace or a customer-extraction channel.

Its central commercial thesis is:

```text
content growth
+ trademark commerce
+ service opportunity
+ customer follow-up
+ capability development
= daily professional operating value
```

Lite begins with the user's day rather than the platform's database. It should help a Workplace decide what deserves attention, convert governed professional objects into useful content and commercial action, preserve customer and asset authority, and record evidence from each action so tomorrow's decisions improve.

## Editorial standard

Each substantial chapter should explain:

1. the practical business problem;
2. why ordinary dashboards, content tools, CRMs or marketplaces do not solve it;
3. the specific Lite Product proposal;
4. the objects, roles and authority boundaries involved;
5. normal, exceptional and failed operating paths;
6. implications for Product design, data, AI, permissions and economics;
7. customer, asset, professional and platform risks;
8. unresolved decisions that require later Product or legal governance.
