## C. Case portfolio

Create a coherent set of fictional, synthetic, public or authorized cases.

Recommended cases:

1. **Daily operating surface:** a small agency balancing customer deadlines, content and buyer demand.
2. **Marketing Pack:** a verified Class 11 trademark transformed into concept assets with clear labels.
3. **Private supply:** a private Inventory item matched to a selected buyer Request.
4. **Multi-party transaction:** seller agent, buyer agent, co-broker, reviewer and MarkReg route.
5. **Failed transfer:** missing authority or official rejection requiring recovery.
6. **Maintenance Lead:** public signal verified, relationship checked and converted into customer instruction.
7. **False entity resolution:** similar company names incorrectly merged and later separated.
8. **Capability growth:** guided preparation, supervised production and later independent status.
9. **Paid Contribution:** visual or data review with scope, revision and compensation.
10. **Cross-Product flow:** Lite content to Sites, Inquiry back to Lite, Service Handoff to MarkReg.

Each case should show state transitions, authority and evidence—not only narrative success.
