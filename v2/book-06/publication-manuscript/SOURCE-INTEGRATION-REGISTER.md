## 4. Historical and frozen-source limitation

The Frozen Book 06 baseline has not been restructured or overwritten during this batch. A complete line-by-line historical source reconciliation remains pending.

Accordingly, current source-retention claims mean:

- all located v2 source themes are being mapped;
- no claim is made that every sentence or case from the Frozen Book has already been reconciled;
- final publication replacement cannot be considered until the historical source audit is completed.
