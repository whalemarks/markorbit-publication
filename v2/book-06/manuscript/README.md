## Product thesis

Lite is the daily operating product for independent trademark professionals and small teams. It combines content production, trademark inventory, buyer demand, transaction opportunity, service leads and capability growth in one Workplace experience.

## Product boundary

Lite is not:

- a smaller MarkReg;
- a generic AI content generator;
- a public marketplace without customer protection;
- a CRM replacement detached from trademark work;
- a provider directory;
- a legal service operator by default.

Lite discovers, organizes and advances opportunity. Formal lifecycle delivery may move to MarkReg or MGSN through governed Handoff while the originating Workplace retains its legitimate relationship role.
