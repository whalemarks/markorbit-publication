## 6. Controlled change policy

During publication review:

### Allowed without architecture reopening

- grammar and style correction;
- terminology normalization;
- removal of redundant prose;
- cross-reference insertion;
- figure/table addition;
- legally safer qualification;
- citation addition;
- reader-facing examples that do not alter Product semantics.

### Requires explicit architecture review

- new chapter;
- new participant or authority role;
- change to Provider appointment logic;
- change to funds or settlement authority;
- change to formal-state ownership;
- change to Professional Qualification or Authority boundaries;
- change to Customer or Workplace sovereignty;
- change to the three route model;
- new implementation or production claim.
