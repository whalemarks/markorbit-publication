## 6. Editorial source treatment

Every substantive claim should be treated as one of:

```text
Accepted architecture
Frozen Product baseline
Generalized operating observation
Illustrative scenario
Candidate future design
Implementation prerequisite
External fact requiring citation
```

The manuscript should signal the category where confusion would create authority or factual risk.
