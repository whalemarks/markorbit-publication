## 3. Chapter review

## CH29 — Network Effects Must Increase Reliability, Not Lock-in

### Purpose

Explains which network effects are legitimate and how MGSN can increase reliability without trapping participants, relationships, data or Matters.

### Main distinctions

```text
More participants
≠ better network

Platform-mediated introduction
≠ platform ownership of the relationship

Matter portability
≠ Provider directory export

Data ported
≠ authority transferred

Retention by value
≠ retention by friction
```

### Contribution

- distinguishes reliability-producing network effects from dependency-producing lock-in;
- makes supply concentration and correlated downstream dependencies visible;
- preserves Workplace customer relationships and Provider identity;
- allows bounded non-circumvention and attribution without permanent exclusivity;
- defines role-scoped Matter portability;
- prevents portability from exporting unrelated Provider, Trust or procurement data;
- separates data continuity from professional and customer authority;
- treats exit as a governed event with open-work, funds, documents, dispute and retention review;
- separates real transition work from artificial platform exit penalties;
- limits retained data to accountable, purpose-specific uses.

### Review result

PASS.

The chapter strengthens Workplace sovereignty and anti-lock-in controls without turning the platform-owned network into a participant-owned Provider directory.

## CH30 — Cross-border Control Needs Data, Authority and Jurisdiction Boundaries

### Purpose

Makes visible the multiple data, professional, authority, funds, document and dispute jurisdictions present in one global engagement.

### Main distinctions

```text
One Engagement
≠ One Jurisdiction
≠ One Data Rule
≠ One Authority Source

Provider needs context
≠ Provider needs entire customer file

Platform coordinates the engagement
≠ platform owns professional confidentiality

Qualified somewhere
≠ authorized here

Contractual forum
≠ universal jurisdiction
```

### Contribution

- defines a multi-dimensional jurisdiction map;
- preserves purpose-limited and progressive disclosure;
- separates data categories by risk and role;
- governs conflict-check identity disclosure;
- separates platform-visible operational metadata from restricted professional content;
- binds professional authority to exact action, jurisdiction, appointment and mandate;
- governs original-document custody and courier evidence;
- separates payer, invoice issuer, contractual payee, beneficiary and official-fee recipient;
- exposes additional risk created by cross-border subcontracting;
- preserves external legal, regulatory and disciplinary forums;
- limits AI from creating legal bases, waiving confidentiality or approving cross-border transfer.

### Review result

PASS.

The chapter provides a cross-border control framework without making jurisdiction-specific legal claims or asserting universal platform authority.

## CH31 — Operating Metrics Must Measure Completion and Recovery

### Purpose

Defines how MGSN should measure actual capability rather than superficial activity, volume or speed.

### Main distinctions

```text
Network activity
≠ network capability

Provider says done
≠ engagement complete

New Provider chosen
≠ continuity restored

Fewer reported incidents
≠ fewer actual problems
```

### Contribution

- maps metrics to the complete operating chain;
- distinguishes route, engagement, Milestone, Return and reconciliation completion;
- defines route-formation, Allocation, Acceptance and Instruction quality metrics;
- measures fulfillment and Evidence separately;
- includes correction, replacement and recovery metrics;
- separates commercial, funds, Trust, incident and dispute measures;
- identifies metric-gaming risks and denominator distortion;
- requires service, jurisdiction, urgency and risk segmentation;
- treats customer satisfaction as useful but insufficient;
- positions metrics as inquiry inputs rather than automatic causation or sanction;
- proposes a balanced five-question MGSN scorecard.

### Review result

PASS.

The chapter prevents transaction growth and headline turnaround from substituting for completion, Evidence and recovery quality.
