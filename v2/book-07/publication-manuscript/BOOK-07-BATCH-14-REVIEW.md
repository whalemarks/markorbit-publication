
## 6. Boundary review

### Provider accountability

PASS.

Allocation remains pending until explicit Provider Acceptance. Silence and historical practice do not create responsibility.

### Customer and Workplace authority

PASS.

Provider Acceptance does not become Customer Instruction or protected-action approval.

### Version control

PASS.

Instructions, approvals and documents remain version-bound and historically preserved.

### Relationship sovereignty

PASS.

Direct communication does not transfer customer ownership or authorize unrelated solicitation.

### Professional independence

PASS.

The Provider retains professional judgment and may refuse unsafe work.

### Data control

PASS.

Conflict, delivery and supporting-participant disclosure remain purpose-limited.

### Multi-party delivery

PASS.

Supporting participants are permitted, but material substitution, qualification, conflict, funds and data changes must be disclosed and governed.

### Funds authority

PASS.

A new beneficiary is treated as a material route signal. No payment custody or automatic release authority is created.

### Formal-state authority

PASS.

Messages, acknowledgements and Provider assertions do not update official or formal state by themselves.
