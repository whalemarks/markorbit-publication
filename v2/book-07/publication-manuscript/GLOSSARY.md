# Glossary

This glossary is reader-facing editorial apparatus for Book 07. It explains terms as they are used in the manuscript. It does not replace shared Core semantics, create new authority or activate production behavior.

## Accountable Route

The complete service path joining an accountable Provider Organization, an admitted Provider Supply Capability, a valid Service Package, current Eligibility, explicit Provider Acceptance, versioned authority, observable fulfillment, attributable Evidence and a usable correction or recovery path.

**Primary chapter:** CH32  
**Related terms:** Candidate Route Set, Eligibility, Service Package, Provider Acceptance  
**Do not confuse with:** a Provider name, a directory listing or a Recommendation alone

## Capability

A stable, governed description of an Outcome that can be produced through bounded Skills, Actions or Invocations.

**Primary cross-book source:** Books 01–03  
**Related terms:** Provider Supply Capability, Source Capability, Verified MO Capability  
**Do not confuse with:** current supply, professional status or permission to act

## Capability Claim

A Provider’s assertion that it can supply a defined Capability in a stated context. The claim requires Evidence and governance before it becomes admitted Provider Supply Capability.

**Primary chapter:** CH06  
**Do not confuse with:** MGSN Qualification, Eligibility or Matter-specific Authority

## Capability Need

A purpose-limited representation of the external capability required by an Originating Workplace for a particular business situation.

**Primary chapter:** CH09  
**Related terms:** Need Projection, minimum necessary disclosure  
**Do not confuse with:** the complete customer file

## Candidate Route Set

A bounded set of materially different Eligible Routes presented for user decision.

**Primary chapter:** CH11  
**Do not confuse with:** a Provider directory, search results or a public ranking

## Capability Governance

The authority and process responsible for evaluating, versioning and changing shared Capability definitions.

**Primary cross-book source:** Books 01–03  
**Do not confuse with:** MGSN Trust adjustment, Provider admission or one Product’s local learning

## Eligibility

A current decision that a particular Provider Organization, Provider Supply Capability and Service Package may be considered for an exact Need now.

**Primary chapter:** CH10  
**Do not confuse with:** general qualification, availability in the past or Provider Acceptance

## Evidence

Attributable material supporting an assertion, milestone, decision or state.

**Primary chapters:** CH08 and CH22  
**Do not confuse with:** an unsupported statement, an AI inference or formal state by itself

## External Protected Action

An external act that requires specific authority, review and control because it may create legal, financial, professional, public or official consequences.

**Primary chapter:** CH14  
**Do not confuse with:** ordinary preparation, internal drafting or Recommendation

## Incident

A governed review of an event that may create material risk, harm or control failure and therefore requires severity assessment, containment, Evidence preservation and due process.

**Primary chapter:** CH26  
**Do not confuse with:** a Complaint, proven misconduct or a final sanction

## Instruction Package

The versioned, attributable handoff stating exactly what a Provider may prepare or do, under which scope, authority, document set, deadlines, funds conditions and Return requirements.

**Primary chapter:** CH14  
**Do not confuse with:** a reusable Service Package or an email thread

## MGSN

The Mark Global Service Network: the managed professional fulfillment network described in Book 07.

**Primary chapters:** CH03 and CH04

## MGSN Connection

The controlled boundary through which a Workplace projects bounded Needs into MGSN and receives governed Returns.

**Primary chapters:** CH04 and CH09  
**Do not confuse with:** ownership of the Workplace’s customer, Matter or internal Knowledge

## MGSN Qualification

Network permission for a Provider Organization to offer a specific Provider Supply Capability under current MGSN rules.

**Primary chapter:** CH08  
**Do not confuse with:** Professional Qualification, current Eligibility or Matter-specific Authority

## MGSN Trust

Service-specific, Evidence-backed, time-bound operating confidence used to improve routing, controls and review.

**Primary chapter:** CH25  
**Do not confuse with:** Capability Verification, professional status, current Eligibility or a public Provider score

## Milestone

An observable fulfillment stage with an expected result, Evidence requirement, deadline or dependency and acceptance criteria.

**Primary chapter:** CH21  
**Do not confuse with:** reported activity or elapsed time

## Originating Workplace

The Workplace projecting the Need while retaining its customer relationship, internal Matter context, private pricing and business decisions.

**Primary chapters:** CH04 and CH09

## Owning Service

The service responsible for validating and maintaining formal business state.

**Primary chapters:** CH04 and CH22  
**Do not confuse with:** MGSN, a Provider Return or an official authority

## Professional Authority

Authority to perform a professional act within the applicable scope, jurisdiction and mandate.

**Primary chapter:** CH08  
**Do not confuse with:** Capability, platform certification, MGSN Qualification or technical access

## Professional Qualification

An externally grounded status recognized by an applicable professional, regulatory or legal authority.

**Primary chapter:** CH08  
**Do not confuse with:** MarkOrbit Capability Certification or Provider admission

## Provider Acceptance

The Provider’s explicit commitment to the exact engagement conditions and Instruction Package version.

**Primary chapter:** CH13  
**Do not confuse with:** Provider Allocation, customer instruction or protected-action approval

## Provider Allocation

A pending request asking a Provider to consider and respond to a defined route.

**Primary chapter:** CH13  
**Do not confuse with:** appointment or Acceptance

## Provider Network Profile

MGSN’s governed representation of an Organization as potential network supply.

**Primary chapter:** CH05  
**Do not confuse with:** the Organization itself or a universal public profile

## Provider Organization

The accountable Organization supplying or proposing to supply a service through MGSN.

**Primary chapter:** CH05

## Provider Return

A typed package returned by a Provider for a milestone, exception, correction or closure, containing assertions, Evidence, Unknowns and required follow-up.

**Primary chapter:** CH22  
**Do not confuse with:** Official Truth, formal-state Validation or Capability Verification

## Provider Supply Capability

A governed MGSN supply projection describing what an accountable Provider Organization may be able and willing to deliver under stated conditions.

**Primary chapters:** CH07 and CH08  
**Do not confuse with:** User Capability Profile, Source Capability, Verified MO Capability or current Eligibility

## Recommendation

An explainable preferred route among Eligible alternatives.

**Primary chapter:** CH12  
**Do not confuse with:** Provider Allocation, Provider Acceptance, appointment or customer instruction

## Relationship Provenance

Evidence of how a participant relationship originated and which bounded protections, preferences and communication rules apply.

**Primary chapters:** CH05 and CH29  
**Do not confuse with:** routing monopoly or ownership of another party’s customer

## Service Package

A reusable, admitted baseline defining scope, exclusions, assumptions, cost layers, deliverables, Evidence and Return expectations for a Provider Supply Capability.

**Primary chapters:** CH07 and CH17  
**Do not confuse with:** an engagement-specific Instruction Package

## Source Capability

A provenance-bound method or capability derived from a particular source.

**Primary cross-book source:** Books 01–03  
**Do not confuse with:** Verified MO Capability or Provider Supply Capability

## User Capability Profile

A person-held, Evidence-based representation of bounded proficiency, experience and development context.

**Primary cross-book sources:** Books 04 and 06  
**Do not confuse with:** Provider Organization supply, employer ownership or Professional Qualification

## User Disposition

The user’s decision to confirm, reject, question or rematch a Recommendation.

**Primary chapter:** CH12  
**Do not confuse with:** Provider Acceptance

## Verified MO Capability

A governed MarkOrbit Capability version that has passed the applicable Capability evaluation and version-control process.

**Primary cross-book source:** Books 01–03  
**Do not confuse with:** Source Capability, Provider Supply Capability or one successful Outcome

## Workplace

A sovereign operating context that retains its own customers, Knowledge, permissions, obligations and Product-local business state.

**Primary chapter:** CH04  
**Primary cross-book source:** Book 04
