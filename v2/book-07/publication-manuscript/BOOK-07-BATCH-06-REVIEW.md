## 7. Failure and recovery coverage

The batch substantively covers:

- “filed” without Evidence;
- hidden approval dependencies;
- Provider silence near a deadline;
- incomplete or misattributed Returns;
- Provider assertions conflicting with official Evidence;
- corrected Returns overwriting history;

- official-record correction remaining unproven;
