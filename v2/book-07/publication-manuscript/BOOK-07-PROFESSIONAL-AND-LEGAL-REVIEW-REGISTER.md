# Book 07 v2 — Professional and Legal Review Register

## 1. Purpose

This register identifies manuscript topics that require external professional, legal, financial-regulatory or data-governance review before Final Publication Freeze.

It does not request legal opinions inside the manuscript and does not convert unresolved issues into Product authority.

## 2. Review states

```text
NOT STARTED
ASSIGNED
UNDER REVIEW
CLARIFICATION REQUIRED
REVISION REQUIRED
REVIEWED WITH QUALIFICATION
CLOSED
```

## 3. Review classes

| Code | Review class | Intended reviewer |
|---|---|---|
| PR | Professional practice | Experienced cross-border trademark/IP professional |
| LR | Legal/regulatory | Qualified legal reviewer in the relevant field |
| DR | Data/privacy | Cross-border data and privacy reviewer |
| FR | Funds/financial regulation | Payments, client-money or financial-regulatory reviewer |
| ER | Editorial risk | Senior editor with professional-services publishing experience |

## 5. Evidence expected from review

Each review should return:

- reviewer role and relevant qualification;
- scope reviewed;
- date and manuscript version;
- comments tied to chapter and passage;
- required revisions;
- unresolved jurisdictional limitations;
- final status;
- whether another specialist review is required.

## 6. Review outcome rules

A review may result in:

```text
No Change
Terminology Clarification
Jurisdiction-dependent Qualification Added
Reader Warning Added
Passage Narrowed
Passage Removed
Figure or Table Revised
External Citation Required
Specialist Review Required
```

The review must not silently activate Product, legal or financial authority.

## 7. Publication gate

Final Publication Freeze requires:

- all high-risk items reviewed or explicitly deferred with a publication-safe qualification;
- no unresolved language implying platform-created Professional Qualification;
- no unresolved language implying automatic appointment or customer instruction;
- no unresolved funds-custody or escrow implication;
- no unresolved claim that Provider Return equals Official Truth;
- no unresolved claim that MGSN decides legal liability, discipline or official state.

Until these conditions are met, Book 07 remains a controlled publication-review candidate rather than a final edition.
