## Authority review

PASS.

The revision does not grant MGSN escrow, custody, money-transmission, payment-release, professional-acceptance or formal-state authority.

Finance remains authoritative for accounting and payment execution. Professional and customer approvals remain role-specific. The Owning Service remains responsible for formal Matter state.

## Commercial integrity review

PASS.

- Margin cannot override Eligibility.
- Official fees remain separate from Provider and platform revenue.
- Unknown does not become zero.
- Provider invoices do not prove performance.
- Beneficiary changes require renewed verification.
- Additional work does not automatically justify an additional customer charge.
- Cancellation preserves completed work and official consequences.
