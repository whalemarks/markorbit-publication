## Network thesis

MGSN is the platform-owned and platform-governed global professional service network. It connects authorized demand from a Workplace with qualified external Provider Organizations through controlled routing, procurement, instruction, fulfillment, evidence return, recovery and settlement.

It is not an open directory, unrestricted participant graph or lowest-price bidding market.
